### Description
This style adds a slight transparency to the popup menus & tooltips in LavaFox & BlackFox Firefox Themes. 
https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/user/4710003 
It should work with any theme so feel free to try. 
It's easy to change the amount of transparency by editing the opacity values, less opacity = more transparency.
