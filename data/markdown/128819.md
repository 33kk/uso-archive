### Description
This is a remake of Melanie Hoffs "DECODELIA" ( http://www.artdelicorp.com/2016/03/01/decodelia/ )
It allows to hide what's on your screen from everyone except you with your red glasses (like one eye of these old school 3D-glasses).

Firefox only, breaks when iframes appear.
