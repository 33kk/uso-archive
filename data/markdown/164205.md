### Description
A simple dark theme for the new infinite campus student interface

!!!Make sure to click "customize settings" and set it to the base url for your school district's infinite campus, i.e. campus.appling.k12.ga.us or nye.infinitecampus.org!!!

To get to the new interface from the old one, click the "Try the new Campus Student" button in the top right.

Use Stylus, not Stylish for installing these themes: https://add0n.com/stylus.html
