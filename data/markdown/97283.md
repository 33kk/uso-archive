### Description
[UPDATED FOR THE NEW TWITTER LAYOUT]

This style will remove the little username text on every tweet, perfect for anyone who uses twitter on their phone with usernames hidden. See screenshot for example. Badges like Protected Account will be remain visible, and all link-decorations are cleared out (i.e. no underlining).

If you prefer the other way around (hides fullname instead), get <a href=http://userstyles.org/styles/100009/>this</a> instead~
