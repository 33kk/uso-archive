### Description
Changes font on Duolingo from Museo Sans Rounded (which does not properly support ș, ț, or ă) to Pontano Sans (which has full support for the Romanian alphabet, and retains the style and feel of Museo Sans Rounded.)  Pontano Sans is available from Google Fonts (https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Pontano+Sans?selection.family=Pontano+Sans) and MUST be installed on your computer.
