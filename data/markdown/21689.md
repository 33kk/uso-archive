### Description
Unlike other auto hide Menubar, this shows up only if the mouse is just under firefox titlebar, minimizing the unrequested movement of the top bar.

Now compatible with Firefox 4.0

If the menubar is not showing up when you go under firefox's title bar, try to increase the "-23" value (like "-20" or more)
