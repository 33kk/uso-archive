### Description
Description:
 Rounded gray popups to integrate with Simple-Gray Styles.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
What it does:
 - makes the popups light-gray and rounded
 - makes the Personal-Bookmarks popups BLACK and rounded
 - makes all popups a little transparent
 - makes the popups font small & bold
 - makes the separator dotted
 - gives a blue highlight effect to all popups
 - integrates with the other Simple-Gray styles
