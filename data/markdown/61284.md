### Description
This style will remove the new black/white Google Bar that Google introduced in late 2011 for only Google Calendar.

The goal is to increase vertical space for Google Calendar Month View (which I mainly use) and expand the hidden appointments in "+more" link. It will not touch Google Calendar sidebars on the left/right side of the screen (mini calendar & task).

Work best If you choose "Compact" display density in the top-right Gear icon.
