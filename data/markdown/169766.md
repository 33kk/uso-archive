### Description
Removes the sidebar ad-banners and maximizes the game-content to the window-size, giving it a more cleaner look, and making it more playable when using smaller window-sizes. 

Works for most game-websites by 247games.com, like: 247blackjack.com, 247solitaire.com, 247sudoku.com, 247mahjong.com, 247hearts.com, 247spades.com, 247checkers.com, 247roulette.org, 247videopoker.org, 247slots.org, 123freecell.com; including their Holiday-special websites like (for example) christmas-solitaire.com, valentinesdaymahjong.com, etc.

Please provide feedback if you want your favourite 247-game url added to the list if it isn't already.
