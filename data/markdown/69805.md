### Description
Removes the clutter for people, who don't quote or report often. Also removes most of the user info, so you'll have to look at their profile for info.

Use with Geekhack Point and Click PM http://userstyles.org/styles/69805/geekhack-simple-ui?r=1342912567 to still be able to easily send PMs.

Read thread titles achieved with Geekhack AutoPilot Greasemonkeyscript, get it here http://userscripts.org/scripts/show/114458

You can hide profile pictures and signatures in your account settings at geekhack.
