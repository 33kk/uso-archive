### Description
Changes the layout of http://online-sweepstakes.com to a pretty black and green layout, with some useless things removed, such as the footer.

You can edit the code to choose exactly what you want to get rid of, and here's a list of things you can remove. :)

div#footer - Footer
div#cfooter - "Checked and valiadated"
div#time - Time and date in the corner (By the log out button)
div#searchctnr - Search
div#sortmenu - Sort Menu (Newest first, etc.)
div#navtop - Nav bar at top
div#navbottom - Nav bar at bottom and [top] link
div#breadcrumbs - [Date] [Number of sweeps] [Dropdown] at top left

span.sweepnum - Sweep number (Next to red/green circle)
p.stitle - Contest Title
span.sfreq - Number of times you can enter (1x per person, daily, etc)
span.smisc - How you enter (Comment, trivia, email entry, etc.)
span.serc - Post note, Rate, Report, Link
p.scmmts - Comments (Not the prizes box)
a.serc - Archive
span.selig - Who can enter (Area and age)
span.sexp - Expiration Date
span.sdate - Submit Date
