### Description
Planning Center Online is a suite of applications for church administrators to manage all the aspects of the church. Check-ins is one of those applications that deals with attendance. This style aims to bring this application into the future. Some of Check-In's app sibling have a more updated look, and this style tries to follow in line with them, as well as add some useful tweaks.
