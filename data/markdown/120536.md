### Description
A minimal Doctor Who Style for everyone and every site.
Based upon the Doctor Who cursor theme (https://userstyles.org/styles/83152/doctor-who-cursor) with a cyan loading background and a Tardis pattern as site background.
Sometimes affects the whole site, sometimes the footer only.
Works mostly with other styles.
Tested with Chrome/Vivaldi
