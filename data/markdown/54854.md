### Description
(1) Collapse the header and footer into thin horizontal strips at the top and bottom of the page; they expand and reappear when the mouse hovers over them.
(2) Move the sidebar on the right of the page to the left and collapse it into a thin vertical strip at the left edge of the article; the sidebar expands and reappears when the mouse hovers over it.
(3) Move the article "filmstrip" from the top of the page to the bottom.
(4) Remove the social networking links at the top of the page (those below the article remain)
