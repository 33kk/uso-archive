### Description
Use&nbsp; "Color Toolbar(s)"&nbsp; to help identify an additional profile such as might be used with a TEST profile,  or  to distinguish a different user, or version of Firefox with a unique color choice per profile.

Choose a color which will be used in your profile on the  Firefox Window, on Customize Toolbars, the  Dom Inspector,  Organize Bookmarks  (Library), Stylish Manage &amp; Edit, and several other dialogs choosing a different color for each profile, some suggestions for light pastel colors are within the code.
