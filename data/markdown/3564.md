### Description
* Make article content in the full view use better fonts (Georgia for titles, Lucidia Grande for body), 
* limit content to 40em's wide
* reposition the "posted on, updated on" making it easier to track for people who care about it. 
* Rescales images that are wider than the available width, which is handy for photoblogs and comics.
* Adds some whitespace to full view to make it easier to read
