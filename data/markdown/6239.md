	-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

	Instructions:

	*Set your forum(s) in the domain field,  i.e : domain("forums.gentoo.org")
	*Set your date syntax to " d M y, G:i  "

	Optional:

	* Install Snap Links Firefox add-on   ( http://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/4336 )
