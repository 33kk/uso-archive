### Description
<b>NOTE: As of 01.01.2013 this style will no longer be supported.</b>
Create your own Custom "Real" Post Counts for Posts, Followers and Drafts. Change one, two or all three!
<b>NOTE:</b> Actual post statistics will display upon hover.

"Fake" your counts or use a custom text message. Custom Counts color can also be selected to match your Post Text (only affects Custom Count text). With the addition of the <a href="http://userstyles.org/styles/79421/tumblr-custom-real-posts-text">Custom "Real" Post Text</a> style, a variety of different effects can be achieved.
NOTE: Even if not in use, at least 1 character must be entered in all "<i>Provide your own</i> URL" fields.
Your Custom Count length expands to the <i>left</i> of the default count placeholder. This may cause your Custom Count to overlap your Post Text.
