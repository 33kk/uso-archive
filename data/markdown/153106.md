### Description
A dark grey/black theme for Yahoo Philippines, in particular, pages on the domains ph.yahoo.com and ph.news.yahoo.com. Colorization is intended as the site's 'night mode' so reading articles can be a bit easier on the eyes. Planned update to fix inconsistent styling of the overlay when clicking on an article from a news category page. Screenshots will also be updated.
