### Description
Light (#f2f2f2) style inspired by Microsoft Edge. <br>Requires Aero Glass (Windows 8 extension works too), Hide Titlebar Plus (https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/13505/) with specific settings and Simple White (http://www.louis.hk/whitefox/) to work correctly. <br>
Tested on Windows 8.1 (worked flawlessly)<br> Mac OS X (white bar instead of semi transparent Aero + little tweaking in <br>#main-window:not([chromehidden*="toolbar"])[sizemode="normal"] section)<br> and Arch GNU/Linux (same as OS X).
<br>Works the best with light wallpapers
