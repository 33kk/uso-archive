Hides old stickied threads from the following forums so you don't have to keep scrolling through them:
*General Questions
*Great Debates
*Cafe Society
*The Game Room
*In My Humble Opinion
*Mundane Pointless Stuff I Must Share
*The BBQ Pit

Please note that this script is updated manually to account for new thread IDs as they become old (more than a few weeks or months).
