### Description
Adds many customisation options and an animated background to your Google Search.

For 6 images, smooth slideshow animation (using CSS3 keyframes).

<b>Customisation options:</b>
<ul><li>Change the different background slides</li>
<li>Duration for slideshow in seconds</li>
<li>Change the colour and transparency of the footer and its links</li>
<li>Change the colour and transparency of the search bar and search buttons</li>
<li>Hide/Show the voice search icon</li>
<li>Hide/Show the country name below the Google logo</li>
<li>Change the colour of the Gmail and Google image search links</li>
<li>Change the colour of the alert button's background</li>
<li>Enable/Disable changing the alert symbol's size to fit the other buttons</li>
<li>Enable/Disable adding a circle around the "Google apps" button to make it better visible and to fit the other buttons (circle colour is changeable, too)</li>
<li>Adds a customizable hover effect to the user avatar and alert button</li></ul>

### Update notes

<b>Notice 2:</b> Looks best with dark images.
