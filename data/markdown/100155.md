### Description
Why read unuseful (and/or lengthy) troubleshoot instructions when you can mash your F5 key/try again button? This style will remove that text and leaves you with a straight and simple error message. No more lengthy details. Great for gen. tl;dr.

### Update notes
- Added an option to replace the try again button with a message ("press F5 to refresh the page")
