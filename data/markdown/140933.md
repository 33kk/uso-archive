### Description
YouTube's captions ignore your set color if it's unsure about the word it detected, forcing grey colors that may not fit your color scheme. This fixes that.
Notice: This only affects the HTML5 video player.

### Update notes
Fixed problem where your selected color affected unrelated text on the page! Yes, this style <b>only</b> affects the captions now!
