### Description
Little tweaks for Blogger.com Template Editor:

- Enlarged/Heightened TextArea
- Removed unused links, those referred to resetting predefined widgets values, and reverting to classic template.
- Support for Blogger in Draft.

I suggest to install Draft Link, a little Greasemonkey script that let you switch between Blogger and
Blogger in Draft under the dashboard for advanced/beta features.

Links:
Blogger in Draft - http://bloggerindraft.blogspot.com
Draft Link -  http://www.kuribo.info/2008/02/bloggerblogger-in-draft-draft-link.html
