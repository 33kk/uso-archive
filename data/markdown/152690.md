### Description
This is a style for the add-on "<a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/reddit-on-youtube/" target="_blank">Reddit on YouTube</a>", available for Firefox.

Makes text larger, and also makes the colors of the text more conducive for reading on the darker 
background of the add-on.

You can edit the exact values below.

### Update notes
