### Description
I dislike the default blue tint on the toolbars under Windows 7, but I'm a big lover of the LessChrome HD addon. I've created this style in order to pretty things up a bit. This style will also change the look of your Firefox "appmenu button".

This style will also remove the transparency and dropshadow from the Firefox address bar that is caused by installing LessChrome HD.
