### Description
With the new campaign of Yahoo (It's you), Yahoo added a super ugly from Yahoo text to the Flickr logo. This style switches the new logo for the old one. Without "from Yahoo". 

Install this style en live a long, happy life without seeing "from yahoo" on Flickr. Simple as that!
