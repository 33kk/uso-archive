***How to change background or chat background for the one i prefer?***
     1. Go to https://imgur.com/upload and upload you image
               - hover your cursor on "▽" icon in top right corner and click "Get share link" Copy the  BBCode one without [img] [/img]
     2. Go to youtube site
     3. Click on Stylish icon > Installed > find this style > click on pencil "✎EDIT" icon
     4. Change imgur link where /* YOUTUBE BACKGROUND */ or /* CHAT BACKGROUND */ 
                 comments are
     5. Click Save on the left
     6. DONE, you made it (It will restore to default if you update/reinstall style)
