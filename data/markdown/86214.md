### Description
Paragraph numbers for your HTML eBooks! This joyous little script adds paragraph numbers to the paragraphs of your favourite eBooks, providing they're coded sensibly This will aid in referencing particular passages in the absence of page numbers. You can switch the script on to show the paragraph numbers inside any site you're viewing and off once you're sick of seeing them.

You can make derivative scripts however must include a link back to this page or furgelnod.com
