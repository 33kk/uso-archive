### Description
Compress the Search Bar until you hover your mouse over it. 

Thanks for the code, because this style was elaborated on style 1200 by user 939

Features:
+ Search bar is compressed until your mouse hovers over the searchbar
+ Search bar is transparent until hovered.
+ Awesome Bar is lengthened until the searchbar is hovered over (increases the width of the Awesome Bar)
+ Width of the Search Bar is compressed until it has focus

Works on Firefox 3 Only
Gives more Focus to Firefox 3's new Awesome Bar, gives more space as well... wow!
Also works on the new extension, Inquisitor

Integrates with the following styles:
- Style 7959


Works on:
- Firefox3 (Search Bar is Hidden)
