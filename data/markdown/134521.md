### Description
The script takes the challenge of sanitising repubblica.it one step further by updating and extending existing userstyles. The script assumes you are blocking ads because it is 2016.
The "right column" and similar are hidden. All comments to videos and articles are hidden.
Enjoy the news. Only the news.
######



Questo script adotta un approccio radicale alla pulizia di repubblica.it estendendo quanto fatto da altri script preesistenti.
