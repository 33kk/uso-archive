### Description
<p>
Watch anime in a bright room with a dark theme. May the dark be kinder on thine eyes. (Masteranime dark theme)
</p>

This is a dark theme for masteranime inspired by  
<a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/ft-deepdark/?src=search">FT DeepDark.</a>
</p>
