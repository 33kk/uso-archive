### Description
<p>Allows your tasks to start higher up on the page by condensing the header, removing party-related information/content, and moving Search & Filter into the header. Also makes the header a light grey instead of purple.</p>

<p>Optional: Go to Advanced Style Settings to make the navbar less bright and/or make the header extra compact.</p>
