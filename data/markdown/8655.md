### Description
Give Extensions support for the Simple-Gray Theme.
--------------------------------------------------------------------
What it does:
 - give the awesome-bar the Simple-Gray look
 - makes "GlaZoom" buttons looks better
 - makes "Download-Statusbar" looks better
 - skins the "Hyperwords" Textbox
 - makes the "YASS" icon rounded and transparent
 - makes the default loading-bars and "Fission" bar blue
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Supported Extensions:
 GlaZoom, Download-Statusbar, ADBlockPlus, HyperWords, Fission,
 YetAnotherSmoothScrolling (YASS).
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Notes:
 If you want to skin your firefox you also need the Simple-Gray
 Theme.
--------------------------------------------------------------------

 MBlue Simple-Gray Theme
