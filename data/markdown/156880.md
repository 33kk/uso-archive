### Update notes
<b>Required game settings:</b>
* Side Bar Type: <b>Slide</b>

<b>Customization settings legend:</b>
1. (Center content) Submenu not accounted for (see album screenshot)
2. (Center popups) Warning: might make some elements inaccessible depending on the device's size
3. (Fit crew chat) Fits crew chat in the available space (requires <b>Center content</b>)
4. (Fix header menu position) Keeps header menu in place when scrolling
    • Optional: hides contained banners (use when they won't fit the screen)
5. (Sort summons) Sort summons by uncap level
6. (Rearrange co-op room) Put room buttons on top
7. (Compact summons) Compacts summon cards in the selection page (see album screenshot)
8. (Compact lists) Show 5 items per row instead of 4 (grids end up being 5x4 instead of 4x5, saving some scrolling)
9. (Hide crystal count) Hides crystal count from #mypage and #profile in an attempt to stop you from impulsively wasting instead of saving
10. (Hide quest commentary) Hides Vyrn's commentary on the summon selection screen
