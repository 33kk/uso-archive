### Description
Gets rid of the blurry default image of the tumblr instant messager in exchange for a pink floral image, cyan stars, green and purple abstract shapes, a watermelon peacock feather pattern or the option to use your own (any repeating image will work fine). Comes with or without the default black overlay. Customizable color scheme, text and bubble colors. If you want or need something adjusted, customized, or have any other questions or concerns, feel free to contact me <a href="http://www.eatyourheirs.tumblr.com/ask">@eatyourheirs</a>.
