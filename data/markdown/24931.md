### Description
Update of style 6463 by user 5424

Original add-on page: https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/933

Adds a nice 'finishing touch' to Firefox by providing aesthetically pleasing, rounded edges to the location bar, search bar, find bar, and all manor of other toolbars on your browser...

If you are using Glasser, you don't want this style. Check out style 7065.

Improvements:
-Added compatibility with Personas
-Removed toolbar splitter
-Reduced borders
-Changed dropmarkers to original black ones (Instructions to revert to WellRounded 3 style blue dropmarkers are inside)
-Fixed oversizing dotted outlines on menulists
-Fixed inaccurate blue outlines
