### Description
AKA Discord Hard Mode - with dramatically reduced colours.
Color dampened for subtle office use of discord. 

For DIY edits at your own risk; change any percentage numbers to 100%.
This will change all colours not just to dampened, but to fully grey desaturated.
Lower percentages will return colours to normal.
