### Description
gives you more vertical space in Google's <a href='https://inbox.google.com/'>Inbox</a>

### Update notes
list item spacing can be negative (because Inbox has other style rules that also affect the spacing between list items).
title (& action) bar padding only applies when you're scrolling down an email and the bar is fixed to the top of the screen.
