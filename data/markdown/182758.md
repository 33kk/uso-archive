### Description
Eclipse Redesign shall not only bring back the feeling of the old deviantart, but also help your orientation by making the general appearance smaller and add more colours as separators.

### Update notes
Changes:
- notification page: 
* comments in the look of the old dA
* changed appearance for group stacks of messages and activity feed
* "Original" shows prior message

- watch page:
* thumbs for deviations, status, journal and commissions have been made smaller

- other
* journals have been widened 
* default white background for small deviations has been removed
* shadow behind deviation has been removed
