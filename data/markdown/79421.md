### Description
<b>NOTE: As of 01.01.2013 this style will no longer be supported.</b>
Create your own Custom "Real" Post Text categories for Posts, Followers and Drafts. Change one, two or all three! With the addition of the <a href="http://userstyles.org/styles/79428">Custom "Real" Post Count</a> style, a variety of different effects can be achieved. 
<b>NOTE: </b> Even if not in use, at least 1 character must be entered in all "<i>Provide your own</i> URL" fields.
There is a maximum Custom Text length before automatic clipping occurs. Length varies according to character size.
