### Description
A dark, low-contrast (good for night-time) theme for Omegle.com.
Features: <li>a dark background and pleasing color-scheme,</li>
<li>alternating text-color to differentiate each person,</li>
<li>shortened "Stranger" to "S" and "You" to "Y" for chat consistency,</li>
<li>stripped the page of superfluous and distracting elements (e.g. logo).</li>
