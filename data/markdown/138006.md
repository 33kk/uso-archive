Full screen theatre mode for new YouTube design (<a href="https://www.youtube.com/testtube">https://www.youtube.com/testtube</a>)<br><br>

Similar to <a href="https://userstyles.org/styles/122032/youtube-real-cinema-mode">YouTube Real Cinema Mode</a> and <a href="https://userstyles.org/styles/132367/youtube-real-theater-mode">YouTube - Real Theater Mode</a> but supports the new YouTube redesign.
