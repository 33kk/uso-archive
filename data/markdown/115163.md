### Description
With this userscript you may read Social websites (facebook.com, twitter, urdu wikipedia) in Urdu Nastaleeq Fonts, We are using embeded Google Urdu Noto Font for better Nastaleeq view.
You May download Urdu Fonts <a href="https://www.alislam.org/urdu/font/UrduFonts.exe">here</a> and install in your system.
