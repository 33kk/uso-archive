### Description
For Firefox 6+ and latest Firefox Beta, Aurora, and Nightly builds.

This style replaces the default favicon on New/Blank tabs and bookmarked sites that do not have a favicon when in the Urlbar, on the Bookmarks Toolbar, in the Bookmark folders, and in the Bookmark Sidebar with a Fx Branding logo icon.

Fx/Beta = Orange Firefox logo icon
Aurora = Aurora brand logo icon
Nightly = Nightly brand logo icon

Tested on Firefox 6+ and up to the latest Firefox Beta, Aurora, Nightly builds.

To customize or fix Firefox Beta, Aurora, and Nightly builds, you can also have a look at my other styles: user 61655
