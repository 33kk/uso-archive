### Description
This style expands episode videos to fill all free space inside browser window.

Hover over the top part of the page and the main navigation menu bar will appear semi-transparently.
Scroll down to get to the rest of the items (comments, etc.) on the page.

The style was inspired by the style YouTube Real Cinema Mode: https://userstyles.org/styles/122032/youtube-real-cinema-mode
