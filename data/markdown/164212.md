### Description
A modern theme for Github. Google's Material Design was kept in mind for most of the project. In this design I tried to put their current content in a fresh and modern look without altering the workflow.

<b>Customizations</b>
  -Primary Color
  -Primary <b>Hover</b> Color
  -Dark Mode (Beta)

<b>Github</b>: <a href=“https://github.com/MrRobotjs/Material-Hub”>https://github.com/MrRobotjs/Material-Hub</a>

===================

This style is still a work in progress, it will be continuously getting updates.
The style will be finished over time but until then If you find something that I have missed, please let me know!
