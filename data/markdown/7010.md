### Description
Makes About:Cache display an image of your choice.  About Cache can be accessed via the Location Bar (addressbar), and displays detailed information about your RAM Memory and Hard Drive cache.  Another excellent way to implement a new UI feature for your favorite web browser.
The background color of certain text in this style is White, allowing you to read the text throughout the logo, without difficulty.

Integrates with Style 6982 and if all styles are installed, you can customize the about:blank look, the about:neterror the new-tab's background image as well.

Features:
- Appears on about:cache
- Appears on about:cache?device=disk
- Appears on about:cache?device=memory
- Background of text is set to white, to allow reading through your logo.
- Clean and very appropriate default image, included with style (Firefox logo - shadowed)
- Fully customizable style, for your own enjoyment.

Additional Information:
This Style has been tested on multiple Firefox Themes, and has worked perfectly on each.
*NOTE: Please Clear your Cache and Restart your browser after installing or changing this style, your custom image will need to be cached in order for it to be displayed.

Instructions:
In the CSS, change the website image I provided to the image you wish to display
You may also change the background HEX color to match your image
The images that work best as the background are .JPG and .PNG
