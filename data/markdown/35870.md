### Description
This style:

- Moves the menubutton over to the right
- Moves the tab bar to the left
- Makes the tab bar flush with the top of the screen when maximized (application of Fitt's law)
- Turns the menubutton into a small wrench icon (Silk iconset)

Works best with the Omnibar extension installed.

Credit goes to Wojtekmaj (http://userstyles.org/users/43845) for the code necessary to convert the menubutton into an icon.
