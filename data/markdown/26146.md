### Description
lyrics.wikia.com uses -moz-user-select [1] to disable selection of text on the lyrics pages.
This style makes selection of text possible again by overriding this attribute.

[1] https://developer.mozilla.org/en/CSS/-moz-user-select
See also: https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/show_bug.cgi?id=477152
