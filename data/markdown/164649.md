### Description
Removes the "Discard"-button for certain cards (both in browser- and mobile-mode), so you cannot discard them by accident.
Removes the "Go"-button for certain options, so you cannot play them by accident.

This is the updated script for the Fallen London Redesign, based on the old styles by fallingkitten and gazzien.
