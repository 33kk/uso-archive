### Description
To make the Netzpolitik.org once again readable if you have a big screen and normally use a higher zoom level.  

Centered fixed layout width 950px width.
Different Linkcolor inspired by w3.org.
Removed right outer column because of irrelevance
Made by stefanwienert.net for a better experience of that important German blog.

------German
Netzpolitik.org: 
 * Zentriert anstelle linksbuendig
