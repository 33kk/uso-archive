### Description
A clean redesign of the old Salesforce UI.
It's still your Salesforce, but you will see a real difference.

Readability and design improved by:
- limiting width and centering content
- hiding the sidebar (not so useful)
- clearing background
- flattening colors
- adding a gray background to the main info area of contacts, opportunities & accounts
