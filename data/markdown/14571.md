### Description
This style will change the color of your Gmail, Google Reader, Google Voice, and Facebook tabs whenever you have unread items. It also flashes when you have new chat messages in Gmail. This style is especially useful for users of the FaviconizeTab extension [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3780] and also works well with the Badges on Favicon extension [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/8121]. Works with Firefox 3/3.5
