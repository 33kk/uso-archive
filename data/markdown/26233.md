### Description
Just install/enable this and you will no longer see the menubar in Firefox. Just disable it to unhide it.

NOTE: I would suggest installing one of the following add-ons with it:
Personal Menu(https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3895)
Compact Menu 2(https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/4550
Tiny Menu(https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1455)
