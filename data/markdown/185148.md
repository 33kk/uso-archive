### Description
The FelisFire version of the "<a href="https://userstyles.org/styles/136807/flight-rising-highlight-swipp-baldwin-items-v2">Flight Rising: Highlight Swipp & Baldwin Items v2</b>" by <a href="https://userstyles.org/users/331258">Zoga</a>. 

Patch items, items used in Casey's Crafting, and items used in events are highlighted or outlined.

Items used in Casey's Crafting are back-lit in purple

Patch items are outlined in red.

Event items are outlined in blue. 
Highlighted Event Items are: Candle Wax, Doubloon, Rabbit, and Rabbit Foot.

(Discs, Orbs, Grams, and Easter Eggs are not highlighted.)

NOTE: This does not provide an advantage when gathering. The highlighting/outlining does not show above the panel before you click a square. You cannot see what's under the panels.
