### Description
This style sheet adds numbers to each search result(see screenshots below).
 
You need "AutoPager" or "AutoPagerize" for numbering consistency.
 - AutoPager   : https://addons.mozilla.org/ja/firefox/addon/4925
 - AutoPagerize: http://userscripts.org/scripts/show/8551
 
 
Original Idea is "Google result number": http://userstyles.org/styles/3825 (written in Japanese though...)

Your feedback is always welcome.
