### Description
This might not be the best look for Google Drive, but sure it will be great when you have to use Google Drive in a small window.
這也許不是 Google Drive 的最好外觀，但是當你得在一個小視窗中工作時，它肯定不錯。

### Update notes
One day, I had to part my screen into left and right side to look my explorer and Google Drive at the same time, but the browser window was so small that Google Drive webpage couldn't be controled, so I made this. Actually, I use only 2 hours to do that but more than 5 hours to create this. Hope this is not a waste of time.
有天我得水平分割螢幕以便同時瀏覽，但瀏覽器視窗太小， Google Drive 很難控制，於是我做了這個 Google Drive 的樣式。但是實際上，我只用了兩小時處理我該做的事，卻用了超過兩小時搞這東西。希望這不會浪費時間。
