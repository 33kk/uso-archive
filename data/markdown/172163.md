### Description
This enables ligatures on GitHub by replacing their default font with Fira Code.

<b>Note:</b> This style needs FiraCode available in your system. You may have to restart your browser after installing the font for this style to take effect. You can install FiraCode from their official repo: https://github.com/tonsky/FiraCode
