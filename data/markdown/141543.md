### Description
This is a simple patch to the BlackTube theme by Ingrim4 https://userstyles.org/styles/141275/blacktube 
that adds support for a white YouTube+ Settings icon and white Advanced Options icons
⚠ Original BlackTube theme is required https://userstyles.org/styles/141275/blacktube 
⚠ Requires YouTube Plus extention for Google Chrome
⚠ Does not work on the new YouTube Material design
