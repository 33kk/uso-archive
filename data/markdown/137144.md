### Description
A dark, greyish theme for EDDB and ROSS. It keeps most of the colours (i.e. doesn't switch the the orange-based UI in the game), but it's much easier on the eyes than the default white/blue.

I've tried to keep good contrast-levels on most text/backgrounds.
