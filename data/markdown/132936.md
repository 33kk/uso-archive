### Description
Dark favicons are poorly visible on a dark background. This workaround highlights them according to their luminance.
Designed to use with dark firefox themes like <a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/ft-deepdark/">FT DeepDark</a>, <a href="https://www.mozilla.org/ru/firefox/developer/">Firefox Developer Edition</a> and so on.
