### Description
A light style with a warm background color, and removing some of the most distractive elements (Trending Now, Who To Follow, footer). 

I also apply a custom font: Tenor Sans, from Google Fonts. 
You need to install the font on your system to see it, otherwise you will get Times New Roman.
