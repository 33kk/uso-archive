### Description
Allows you to Customize the hover-color of the toolbar buttons.  When your mouse is hovering over your bookmarks toolbar icons, they will be highlighted in a different custom color.  Increases screen navigation and readability.
Once again, this style supports both Firefox2 and Firefox3.

Applies to:
- Bookmarks Toolbar (text of your bookmarks)
- Navigation Toolbar (if your toolbar buttons have text - Right Click / Customize and Select "Icons & Text")
- Main Toolbar (Text of custom or Extension's buttons)
- Also Applies to EVERY single toolbar button on any window of a popup or pane (Stylish toolbar buttons in the Manage Styles window, for example)

Browser Versions:
- Works on Firefox 3
- Works on Firefox 2

Integrates with Style 7721
