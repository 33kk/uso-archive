### Description
A dark style for fanfiction.net and fictionpress.com, for all those who prefer light text on dark backgrounds for longer reading. Color scheme inspired by the FT DeepDark theme for firefox. Black/dark grey and a few blue/cyan colors.

Copying story text works with this style active. Updates regularly as the site updates, too.
