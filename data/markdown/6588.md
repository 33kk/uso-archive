### Description
This customisation makes sense when the Glasser for Vista extension is installed. It increases the padding of the navigation toolbar so the menu bar and bookmarks toolbar are not touching. The menu bar also has its background removed.
See http://forums.mozillazine.org/viewtopic.php?t=649449 for information about Glasser.
