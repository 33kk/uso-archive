### Description
This is a combination of clutterskull's Comment Boxes and the Dark Reddit style by chromakode updated by Poppeseed.  I've made a few tweaks and fixes to make certain images and buttons work correctly.  You'll want to turn off the custom subreddit styles option in your Reddit preferences to keep them from making things look ugly.
