This style is applied to hotmail, calendar and contacts, and gives them an optimal/minimal look by removing unnecessary elements.

What it does: 
+removes ads
+removes upper menu
+removes footer
+removes msn and quickviews items from the left
+strech everything remaining to the edges
+adds logout button on the left-bottom corner


It has been tested on both Firefox and Chrome with the same results.
