### Description
Many modern websites are localizing their language. The font that they use is not the one we Pakistanis are used read. This style will allow you to transform Urdu font to the one of your choosing. There are many fonts to choose from namely Alvi Nastaleeq, "Jameel Noori Nastaleeq", "Urdu Typesetting",  Alvi Lahori Nastaleeq, "Jameel Noori", "Noto Nastaliq Urdu", "Noto Serif", etc/
