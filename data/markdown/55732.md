### Description
Save space for your location bar!
Displays the "Search bar" and the Icons of the bookmarks toolbar, just under the awesome bar just by hovering on the location bar.  It's just a trick inspired by http://userstyles.org/styles/46252/auto-show-transparent-hovering-bookmarks-toolbar. Thanks!
Process :
- Step 1 : Slip the "Search bar" into the Bookmarks Toolbar
- Step 2 : Allow the display of the Bookmarks Toolbar
- Step 3 : Install the present style
- Step 4 : Adjust as you want the Search bar position and the bookmarks list. You can also adjust the length of the Search Contener.
The present solution uses "Firefox 4 Normal window Tabs in Titlebar" http://userstyles.org/styles/42402/firefox-4-normal-window-tabs-in-titlebar. Thanks again!
