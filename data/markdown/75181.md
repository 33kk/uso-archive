### Description
This style hides "Chat" pane on right side of the window to make the area of mail content wider.
If you hover the right side of the screen, the "Chat" pane will appear.
This style works well with "Vertical Split" mode and "No Split" mode now.

This style is...
- tested (and developed) mainly on Google Chrome.
