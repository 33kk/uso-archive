### Description
DS - Improve Slack.com

<ul><li><strong>increase density</strong> in the right sidebar (all sections) and chat, more member names / chat lines are visible at once</li>
<li><strong>move status indicator</strong> to the left side of member name, more readable than default style</li>
<li>add <strong>vertical scroll to pinned messages</strong> (a MUST HAVE for free Slack teams: long messages pinned for more than 2 days become unreadable)</li>
<li>set a <strong>custom background color to BOT's</strong> (and other ephemeral) messages</li>
<li>set a <strong>custom link font</strong> color/weight/decoration</li>
<li>OPTIONAL - add <strong>divider lines</strong> between messages sent by different people (divider transparency is customizable)</li>
<li>OPTIONAL - enable it only on <strong>specific Slack teams</strong></ul>

Suggestions? <a href="http://fb.com/messages/renatofrota">Send me a message</a>.

<strong>IMPORTANT NOTE - It works much better with <strong>Stylish</strong>.
