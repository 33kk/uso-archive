### Description
Show that you voted in the Tumblr Decision 2016! :)

As part of Tumblr's 2016 "april fools joke" anyone who voted in the Tumblr Decision 2016 received a "I Decided" badge on their icon. It has since been removed. This style will put an "I Decided" badge on all icons on your dashboard.
