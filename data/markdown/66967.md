### Description
<p>When hovering over a tab, show the tab title in the space where the urlbar is.</p>
<p><b>If urlbar is not the first item in your navbar, don't use style 82221, the positioning may be a bit off.</b></p>
<p>Inspired by other quicktip styles which don't work once tabs overflow (at least on OSX).</p>
<p>Tested on Firefox 29+ on OSX & Win7.</b></p>
<p>Other styles from user 142430</p>
