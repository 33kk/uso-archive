### Description
The quotes in Open Sans are hard to distinguish. This is a big problem for Chinese translators, because quotes in Chinese must appear correctly in pairs (“ and ”,‘ and ’). This script replaces Open Sans with Noto Sans, which shows the quotes clearly.
