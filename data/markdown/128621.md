Light Color: SVG images, text
Bright Color: Play buttons, fast forward, borders on navigation

This style will turn your search page into a beautiful galaxy background, as well as change the colors of your Spotify SVGs and text.

Change Log

4/18/17 - Please use my other Spotify Web style. This style does not work on the new updated SWP.

6/17/16 - 
Fixed the bug where you can not log in. Enjoy!

6/15/16 - 
Aware of the bug that doesn't allow you to log in. Fixing that, in the meantime, turn off the style to log in. 

6/8/16 - 
*Please look at additional screenshots for color ideas!!!!
Added style settings for 2 colors, the text color and the svg color. (Can not change the yellow bell or settings yet). I prefer a brighter color for the text and play buttons, and a lighter color for the SVG images on the left side bar. But if you want, mix it up! I find it looks the best to use two shades of the same color.
