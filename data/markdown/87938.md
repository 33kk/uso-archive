### Description
Displays small "paper sheet" icon in the top left corner of any Flickr page with your comments currently displayed. 
Does not work with long comment history, divided into several pages.
IMPORTANT: to make it working, replace the "22271981@N07" with your own Flickr user id (could be found, at least, in address of your buddy icon).
