### Description
Makes About:Credits more fun by displaying your name throughout the credits.

** Similar to user 3's Style, but with more Changes and Customizations.

To access these credits, you can go to Tools/About Firefox, and click Credits
You can also access a similar credits list at about:credits in the location bar

Features:
+ Name on about:credits page
+ Your name before each credits alphabet row
+ Your name in the Version
+ Your name or slogan of choice for the Motto


Integrates with Style 8704, and Style 7047

Works on Firefox 3
Works on Firefox 2
