### Description
This userstyle replaces smilies by their text representation and undoes the formatting of text embedded in *.

For example, Facebook replaces "a * b * c" by "a b c", where the "b" is bold. This userstyles re-inserts the asterixes and removes the bold formatting.

This userstyle is designed for Facebook users who want to exchange formulas.
