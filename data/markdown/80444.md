### Description
tong+ Autohide Toolbar in Full Screen Mode (a.k.a. fuller fullscreen) (tested on SeaMonkey 2.x)

Autohide SeaMonkey toolbar and tabbar in full screen mode. 
In full screen mode, move mouse pointer to top edge to show toolbar. 
Move mouse pointer to bottom edge to show tabbar.
