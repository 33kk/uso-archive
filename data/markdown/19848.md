### Description
Draws the entire Firefox download manager on a piece of glass (see preview). 
No additional add-ons are required (besides Stylish of course).
This requires Firefox 3.5.x or higher (so this will NOT work on Firefox 3.0.x) and an Aero glass enabled version of Windows (eg Vista or Windows 7)
