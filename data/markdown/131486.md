### Description
Like reading articles, but haven't gone outside for the past five years? No problem!

This style aims to make the wiki have a dark theme that is much easier on the eyes. (Okay, it might still make your eyes bleed but it won't be because of bright colors, it's just me being terrible at design.)

If you find something that looks god-awful, please leave me some feedback so I can fix it.
