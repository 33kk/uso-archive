### Description
Modifies the Awesome Bar autocomplete to use Aero glass and highlights content based on its bookmark status.

History/Normal items: White text, black text shadow, no background
Bookmarked items: +Bold text, with green background
Tagged items: +Italic text, with yellow "Gold" background

With Omnibar, the following other entries also present themselves;
Phrase suggestions: Orange/Peach
URL suggestions: Sky blue

As you type, the URLs and page titles will highlight with aero blue letters, complimented by a white text shadow.
