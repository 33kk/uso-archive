### Description
community.arm.com blog content splurges out to fill the full width of the browser window, completely ignoring all lessons about the maximum number of words per line for comfortable reading. This style selects the actual blog content, narrowing it down to a readable column of text, centred in the available page real estate. It lets the rest of the content of blog pages fill the whole browser window.
