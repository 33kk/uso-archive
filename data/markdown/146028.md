### Description
This style makes Kimcartoon less "stale" and boring. Most buttons and boxes now have rounded edges giving a sleeker look. Also, some of the colors on the site have been changed to be less stale.

### Update notes
To use this style you need either the Stylish or Stylus extension.
Stylish:  (<a href="https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/stylish-custom-themes-for/fjnbnpbmkenffdnngjfgmeleoegfcffe?hl=en">Chrome</a> | <a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/stylish/">Firefox</a>)
Stylus:  (<a href="https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/stylus/clngdbkpkpeebahjckkjfobafhncgmne?hl=en">Chrome</a> | <a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/styl-us/">Firefox</a>)
Then just click the big blue 'install style' button towards the top of the page.
