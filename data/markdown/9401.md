### Description
Only displays the buttons on the Update Styles popup when you can actually click the button.

Why show a button that you can't click!?

Buttons Affected:
- Update Selected button
- Never Update Selected button
- Update All Styles button

- Works on Firefox 3
- Works on Firefox 2
