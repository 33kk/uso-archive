### Description
Displays the text icons in the FFFFFFFUUUUUUUUUUUU subreddit without needing to turn on custom styles in your preferences. You might not want custom styles turned on because it makes some subreddits look ugly, but then you miss out on all the fun text icons in the F7U12 subreddit. This style enables them all.
