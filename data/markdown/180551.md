### Description
A transparent, borderless and dark Telegram UI.

<b>Options:</b>
- Custom Background Image (and it's size, position and blurriness) or Color
- Transparency Interface Color
- Messages Bubbles sizes and Joining
- Fullscreen
- Colors (Accent, Text)
- Font Family
- Custom Installed Font Family
