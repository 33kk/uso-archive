### Description
Removes the annoying "Add to Queue" button that hovers over the thumbnail on YouTube when your mouse is over the video thumbnail. 

I always click it accidentally and it annoyed me so this style will remove it. Very simple, one line of code.

Does NOT remove the "Add to Watch Later" button.
