### Description
Minimalistic and lightweight, IRC-like Discord style for <b>compact, light theme</b>.

- Applies only to web-based Discord client.
- <b>"Compact Mode"</b> is recommended
- "<b>"Light Theme""</b>  is recommended
- Guild/server list is hidden by default, hover the mouse over the right side to bring it up
- For <b>Pins, Mentions and Userlist</b> - you need to use <b>hotkeys</b>
