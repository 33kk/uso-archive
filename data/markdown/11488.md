### Description
Elegant style for Userstyles.org.
This style features (feature #2) columns for main page. If you don't want them - take a look at my another style (which I actually prefer) - style 11503

Main Features:
1. Elegant header.
2. Main page categories are separated into two columns
3. Nicer-looking bullets
4. Styles are numbered when looking inside category
5. Styles with average rating 4 - 5 have identifying "star" before it's name.
6. Obsolete styles removed   / credits go to user 911 and her style style 2924 /
7. Screenshots are resized back to it's original size. / thanks to user 838 and his style style 6158 for an idea/. Large screenshots are resized to fit body width.
8. Touch-ups while viewing a style.
9. Three-column display for lists of styles. 

update info:  screenshots in rss will now be resized down to body-width (I forgot to copy it from my style 11912)
