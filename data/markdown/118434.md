### Description
A simple theme that simplifies the irccloud theme: 

- Removes a ton of blue. 
- Removes a lot of borders and contrast. 
- Makes highlights less "in your face" but simply shows a border next to the person that highlighted you. 
- Channels on the left. 
- Chat in monospace 
- Smaller fonts 
- Menu reachable by hovering in the top right corner.
