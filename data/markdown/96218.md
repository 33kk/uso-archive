### Description
Use for bulk commenting.

Autohide mode works on Firefox Android and Desktop browsers, and is now default.

On Android, tap the blank portion of the toolbars to show, tap outside of the comment box to hide.

On desktop browsers, mouseover to show, mouse away to hide.

The comment preview now autohides as well. When you click Preview, a bar will appear, reading "Hover/Tap to View" When you read it, you should do it.
