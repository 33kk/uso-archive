### Description
A style to revert the latest change to GitHub's dashboard/homepage/startpage/notifications with the following options:
- Centered to remove full width
- Centered top bar (optional) 
- Centered notification view (optional)
- Moves the now left bar back to the right where it belongs! (optional)
- Remove discovery (optional)
