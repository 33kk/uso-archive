Add a pretty slick shadow below your tab-bar (above the navigator toolbar) when in a normal window, and an Office 2010-style underglow when in a maximized window.

The screenshots really don't do justice. Try it out.

Tested on Firefox 4 beta 6 nightly on Windows 7 Aero.
