### Description
This style hides the Youtube Red & TV advertising buttons in the sidebar menu that I keep accidentally clicking on because they're right next to 'subscriptions'.

This style is designed for whichever version of Youtube's UI Google decides my account will have on any given week. May not work for other versions of the Youtube UI.
