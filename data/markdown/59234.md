### Description
Google's "Search Plus Your World" forces (often less relevant) Google+ content into your search results sidebar. This simple stylesheet hides that section so you can focus on organic results. Examples of how "Search Plus" adds irrelevant content: http://searchengineland.com/examples-google-search-plus-drive-facebook-twitter-crazy-107554
