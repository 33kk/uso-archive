### Description
Remove the floating buttons that pollute Google Docs' simple interface:
- Hide the circular comment buttons that hover over the right-hand margin
- Hide the "Explore" call-to-action in the bottom-right hand corner
- Hide the lower-right button to show Companion (if you've hidden Companion and want to re-show it, disable Stylus temporarily to access the button)
- Hide the "Outline" button in top left corner
- No more floating message to press Esc to leave full screen, every time you enter full screen
