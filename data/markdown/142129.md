### Description
Modern dark theme for autoblog.nl. For the best experience combine with the 'Autojunk.nl Dark' theme as both sites are part of the same 'network' and deeplink to eachother.  

<b>Features:</b>
&bullet; Total dark make-over while respecting the original brand-styling.
&bullet; A decluttered and more consistent layout; easy on the eyes.
&bullet; Optimized primary-content width and font-styling for improved readability.
&bullet; Round-borders, subtle shadows, transparency and a themed scrollbar make it more slick & modern looking.
&bullet; Removes the large, redundant 'floormat' footer.
&bullet; Effective Ad-suppression even without a dedicated Ad-Blocker plugin.
 &nbsp; It does not block tracking, but does suppress the display of all Ads except Branded Content articles.
