### Description
A few very simple adjustments to The Old Reader to make it look a little more elegant and easier to read:
<ul>
<li>additional padding around entries</li>
<li>larger headlines for better overall hierarchy on larger monitors</li>
<li>slightly larger text and better spacing for readability</li>
<li>margins around images so they don't do strange things to the text</li>
<li>nicer blockquotes, with a subtle gray background and rounded corners</li>
