We love the Google Inbox design. And we want ugly new Gmail looking exactly like that.

R.I.P. Google Inbox. All our thoughts and prayers are with you.

Basic features:

* Smaller font size.
* Better font family.
* Font color adjustments.
* Background color in e-mail listing adjustments.
* More of white space everywhere.
* Dividers between basic sections in left menu.

Optional features. All enabled by default, use "Customize Settings" button to disable some od them:

* Hide unread counts in left menu.
* Hide stars in e-mail listing.
* Hide date and time in e-mail listing.
* Hide scrollbars in e-mail listing.

Suggestions, what to do next in Gmail settings:

* Set Comfortable or Default Display Density.
* Disable categories tabs, priority inbox and all other such crap.
* Disable personal level indicators, importance markers and other such crap.
* Hide all labels you don't want to see.

Feel free to send me any improvement suggestions to jantichy@jantichy.cz!
