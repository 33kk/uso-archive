### Description
Improves Youtube Feather by making the video bigger, thereby triggering it to play in HD automatically.

To enable Feather on Youtube, go here: https://www.youtube.com/feather_beta/

If you want it to be dark, you will find a couple of commented out lines that you could uncomment.

Based on the great userstyle "Youtube Feather HD Black" by kript0x: http://userstyles.org/styles/83807/youtube-feather-hd-black
