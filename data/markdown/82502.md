### Description
Very Simple Style, Forces Browser Input Boxes and Controls
To Be Black Text or Gfx on a White Background. This fixes
problems with your browser when using certain desktop themes
that like to be incompatible with webpages --- or with webpages
that change only text colors not backgrounds...
