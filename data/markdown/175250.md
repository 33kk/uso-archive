### Description
This stylish adds a blue modern design with round edges and blur to youtube.
I hope you like my stylish!

<b>Highlights</b>:
- Rounded edges
- Blurred Backgrounds
- Fade-In Animations
- Socialblade Support

<b>Note</b>: This stylish was made for Youtubes Dark Mode so please <b>enable Youtubes Dark Mode</b>.

<b>Note 2</b>: If you don't see blurred backgrounds, you may need to enable them in your browser-preference. Otherwise your browser doesn't support blurred backgrounds. <a href="https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS/backdrop-filter#Browser_compatibility">More Info</a>

If you have feedback or found some bugs let me hear it in the comments!
