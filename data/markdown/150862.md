### Description
Remove space taken by banner ads on Flash pages + Unity client on Warzone. While AdBlock/uBlock Origin remove the ads, they do not appear to remove the blank space. This acts on the parent div of Warzone's Flash object elements to remove the left offset (taken up by ads) and restore the width to its original value.
