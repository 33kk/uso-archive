### Description
Hides promo banners and ads on Twoo.com.

* Hide promo banners and ads
* Hide credits, gifts and boost icon.
* Indicators do not bounce by default. This can be changed at the bottom of the CSS file.
* Birthday "messages" are hidden by default. This can be changed at the bottom of the CSS file.
