❖ <b>Installation</b>
1. Click the “Customize Settings” button
2. Choose your preferred option from the drop-down menu
(or select the <b>No Change/No</b> option, to not apply an item)
3. Click the “Install” button

❖ <b>Update or change the style settings without reinstalling (with Stylus) </b>
1. After installation, refresh the style page, now you will see an “Update” button
2. Change the settings
3. Click the “Update” button to apply new settings on Feedly
<b>EACH TIME</b>, you can change the settings and apply these new settings with the “Update” button
