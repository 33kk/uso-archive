### Description
Spring feeling dashboard theme for tumblr.

~made by ghostxflowers@tumblr



*Looks better in Google Chrome with a screen resolution of 1280 by 800 pixels*

--------------------------------------------
HOW TO INSTALL
--------------------------------------------
1. Download Stylish;
- Mozilla Firefox users go to: https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2108/
- Google Chrome users go to: https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/fjnbnpbmkenffdnngjfgmeleoegfcffe
2. Click 'Install with Stylish' from the right side menu (the box with green background) then 'Ok'.
3. Go to Tumblr and take a look at your new Dashboard!

--------------------------------------------
HOW TO UNINSTALL
--------------------------------------------
~For Mozilla Firefox users~
1. On the menu on top, click 'Tools' then 'Add-ons'.
2. A box will open up, click 'User Styles'.
3. Click on the name of the Style then click 'Uninstall'; or 'Disable' if you still want to keep it
but doesn't want to use it.

~For Google Chrome users~
1. Click the 'wrench' icon from the menu on the far right end.
2. Click 'Tools' then 'Extensions'.
3. A new window will show up, look for 'Stylish' then click 'Options'.
5. Another new window will show up, click 'Delete' then 'Ok'.
