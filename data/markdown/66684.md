### Description
The header at the top of the page is collapsed to a thin horizontal strip and pinned to the top of the window, the sidebar on the right side of the page is collapsed to a thin vertical strip and pinned to the left edge of the window, and the footer at the bottom of the page is collapsed to a thin horizontal strip at the bottom of the page. Hover over any one of them to expand them again and display them on top of the page content.
