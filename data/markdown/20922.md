### Description
This userstyle emulates the suggested "Progress Line" on tabs in the Firefox 3.7 Mockup Theme Revision 2: https://wiki.mozilla.org/Firefox/3.7_Windows_Theme_Mockups

This style modifies Tab Mix Plus: https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1122 it's required to make it work.

This userstyle was designed to be used in conjunction with the Firefox 4 Mockup Redux3 theme by BoneyardBrew & dert07 at: http://www.neowin.net/forum/index.php?showtopic=800684

Direct download of the Firefox theme http://www.neowin.net/forum/index.php?act=attach&type:post&id=238174
