### Description
More compact rendering of Geocaching.com.

Features:
<ul>
	<li>difficulty, terrain, size and favorites are on the same line;</li>
	<li>favorite points have a flatter look;</li>
	<li>gray boxes are replaced by white boxes;</li>
	<li>all warnings are removed;</li>
	<li>printing box and waypoint downloads are hidden (please contact me if you actually use those, I'll add a setting);</li>
	<li>users' stats are now next to the avatars rather than below (mostly to save space on short logs);</li>
	<li>link to view logs is hidden;</li>
	<li>tables no longer have borders;</li>
</ul>
