### Description
lawnsite.com Retina Saver & Streamlining Style

- Replaces background colors with blue/gray colors 
- Normalizes fonts and links
- Fixes some layout issues (smiles in advanced editor) among others
- Limits posted image sizes to 600px wide in order avoid horizontal scrolling
- Strips ads and images not caught by AdblockPlus and NoScript and attempts to compress the white space left behind
- IMPORTANT: Designed and tested with AdblockPlus and NoScript Firefox extensions enabled
- Intended for use by lawnsite regulars that want a stripped down interface

Covers both orange/brown/red theme and the standard green theme and new 2013 changes.

Validates No Errors Or Warnings CSS 2.1 & 3
