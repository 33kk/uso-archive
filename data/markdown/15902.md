### Description
Completely removes the "Highlights" panel in the right sidebar of the new Facebook homepage layout. Ideal for getting your pokes and events back where they should be near the top of the page!


If you want to remove suggestions, pokes, events or anything else please generate your own style at www.customizefacebook.co.uk - just choose what you want to hide on your Facebook homepage and your code will appear immediately! There is also a group for this style and the Customize Facebook website at www.facebook.com/group.php?gid=74162283330.
