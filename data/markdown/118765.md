### Description
An up-to-date dark theme for the MusicBee Forum that resizes to your screen resolution. You can choose your text and link color of choice! I might add more customizable settings later. Let me know if you have any issues.

I also make music! Check it out: https://soundcloud.com/guitarjorge24

Based on the theme "MusicBee - Dark Dark".
