### Description
Not everyone loves the redesigned lessons. Here's a bit of an old school look, boxed for less negative space, changed colours and font sizes for easier readability and the questions are next to the textarea again and a few other changes.

Discussion on Duo: https://www.duolingo.com/comment/24634938
