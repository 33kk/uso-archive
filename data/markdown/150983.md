### Description
Planning Center Online is a suite of applications for church administrators to manage all the aspects of the church. People is one of those applications that deals with congregants. This style aims to bring this application into the future. Some of People's app sibling have a more updated look, and this style tries to follow in line with them, as well as add some useful tweaks.
