### Description
Want to play YouTube videos in fullscreen, but still want to be able to switch tabs and do other browser things ? Use this style to do just that!
After applying this style, whenever you choose the medium player mode in YouTube, the video will fill up the whole content area, thus giving you only video on the page but leaving your browser in tact to be used as you want.
