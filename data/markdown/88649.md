### Description
A cleaner and simpler style for viewing the 'new flickr'. Removes the banner and reduces the screen-space that the banner used. Provides a light-grey background for the header and photo pages. Moves the pop-up title bar to below the image, rather than partially obscuring it. Also increases the initial size of the text insertion box for comments.
