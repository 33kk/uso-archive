### Description
This script will optimise and remove un-needed and annoying parts of the somethingawful.com forums.  Everything is commented so it is easily customised. If I missed anything or there are any problems, please let me know!

This script does the following:
- Removes ads
- Removes white space so that the page is more compact
- Removes unregistered nags and annoyances
- Corrects some small issues with their styling
- Removes little-used elements (purchase bar etc)
