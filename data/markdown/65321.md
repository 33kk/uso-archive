### Description
I can't seem to get my styles to apply to just tumblr so they appear on every website.
To stop this yourself:

- go into stylish and find the theme
- click edit
- click the box next to 'Applies to' and click 'URLs starting with'
- then enter 'http://www.tumblr.com'
- now save
