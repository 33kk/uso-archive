### Description
Show group discussions in a more compact, easier to read and more manageable style in the new group layout. Also fixes the styles for &lt;blockquote&gt; and &lt;em&gt; that are incorrect in groups. Thread titles are never truncated.

This style only affects group discussions (i.e. "Discussions" tab and left pane in other tabs).
