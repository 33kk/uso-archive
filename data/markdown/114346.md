### Description
Removes everything except the song info, album art, player controls, and progress bar (somewhat inspired by the Pandora mobile app). This style is mostly responsive, and is primarily designed for people who don't change stations often.

Note that you cannot easily change the station or access your account settings while this stylesheet is active; you will need to disable the stylesheet temporarily for these functions.
