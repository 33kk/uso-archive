### Description
Removes items that are nuisances for drive-by "not-my-city, not-a-subscriber" readers from Tribune properties - "Promoted stories", "Share" links, advertising, subscriber-only controls, "Premium Content" teaser links and other self-promotion, "Start Trial" and "Subscribe" nags.

Optionally shrinks the useless lede photo and restores it on hover.
