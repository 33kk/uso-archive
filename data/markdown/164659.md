### Description
Until you hover the left margin, this style hides the left navigation panel. It also centers the main content on a wiki page powered by <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MediaWiki">MediaWiki</a>, see <b>Disclaimer</b> below for exceptions.

Also, when you make the panel show up (by hovering the left margin), it gets a background color so that its contents are still visible even if they overlap with something else.
