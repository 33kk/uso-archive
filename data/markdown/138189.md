### Description
Locks the top navbar, removes the "ads" linking from your current Wikia location to other Fandom pages, including the massive top banner, the footer bar and the sidebar, and makes the articles full-width to counter the empty space created when removing the sidebar. (Primarily released so I can move it to another computer I use.)
