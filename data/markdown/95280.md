### Description
Brighten up your YouTube experience with this custom video progress bar. The subtle yet vibrant glows will bring some life and color to the YouTube HTML5 video player.

If you fancy something cute and animated, try this alternative style: <a href="https://userstyles.org/styles/95033/youtube-nyan-cat-progress-bar-video-player-theme">YouTube - Nyan Cat progress bar video player theme</a>.

### Update notes
If you're unable to change the colours while using Stylish or Stylus, please read <a href="https://forum.userstyles.org/discussion/57282/how-to-change-the-color">this helpful guide</a> on manually changing the colours, courtesy of <b>brndnily</b>.

<strong>This style only works on YouTube videos supporting the HTML5 video player.</strong> You must also have the HTML5 video player enabled on your YouTube account (<a href="http://www.youtube.com/html5">you can do that here</a>).

Please report bugs via the discussion section. Contact: zoiduserstyles [at] outlook {dot} com
