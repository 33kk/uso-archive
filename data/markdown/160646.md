### Description
With this style you can see even more stories on your instagram frontpage!
This enlarges the story container, so that more stories can be chosen from.

Also, you've now got the option (as of v1.3) to remove the "recommended" box!

This shouldn't break other Instagram skins, so you can use this as an addon.
