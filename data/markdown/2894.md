--[help notes]

This is the selector that adds the "http://" to orange search result URLs:
:not(a) > br + font[color="#008000"]:before
If you use "Linkification" scripts/extensions to change non-link URLs into links, change that selector to this:
:not(a) > br + font[color="#008000"] > a:before
So that the "http://" will be part of the link.
