A cleaner and more modern UI for Gmail web app with a taste of material/flat design. Based on https://goo.gl/mY5OdX without the red-ish color.

Requires this extension to work properly:
https://goo.gl/PDcAOg

Set the headear bar off, by configuring the inbox characteristics on the Gmail settings > Gmeilius > Inbox section.
