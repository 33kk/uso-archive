### Description
Dark styles for the <a href="https://add0n.com/stylus.html"><b>"Stylus"</b> Extension/Addon</a> which is far-superior to the <i>"Stylish"<i> extension.

Works on Firefox and Chrome.

Different themes available via "Customize" button.

Overwrites the source editor's "default" theme (leaves other themes alone for the most part).
