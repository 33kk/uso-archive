### Description
A customizable theme for sistema.tamo.lt/tamo.15min.lt/dienynas.tamo.lt. Also disables the sponsored advertisements currently visible on tamo.lt.

Tamo (Tavo Mokykla) is a lithuanian website for schools to sync their student data online, show homework, grades, etc.
