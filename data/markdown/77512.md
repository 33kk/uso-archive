### Description
This style enhances YouTube comments:

1- Automatically expand spam comments
2- Display comments score on bottom right
3- Display "TOP COMMENT" on top right for top comments
4- Show the number of likes/dislikes (up/down) of comments. 
5- Bold and capitalize "SHOW THE COMMENT"
6- Warning colors for spam comments
7- Highlight removed comments in black and white
8- Highlight high ranked comments
9- Dim low ranked comments
10- Show a red left border for comment replies
