### Description
When watching any kind of e-sport event on twitch, a lot of unwanted spoilers can occur just by seeing how long the video goes on for.
Only 10 minutes left when it's 2:0 in a BO5? Guess who wins...
For seeking ahead or back, just focus the video and use the arrow keys.
