### Description
<p>
Write thy themes in the dark. May the dark be kinder on thine eyes. (Stylus dark theme)
</p>

<p>
This is a dark theme for Stylus inspired by  
<a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/ft-deepdark/?src=search">FT DeepDark.</a>
</p>
