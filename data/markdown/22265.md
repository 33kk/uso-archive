### Description
The AutoTOC script for GreaseMonkey (see http://userscripts.org/scripts/show/1301) is a very useful thing. Unfortunately, it always overlays the end of the page, so you cannot see what is hidden behind.

This style now reduces the AutoTOC navigation bar to a small dot in the lower left corner - until you hover that dot, restoring the complete bar.
