### Description
Changes Firefox nav-bar based on the Void Windows style (http://neiio.deviantart.com/art/Void-Windows-10-Edition-569696624).

### Update notes
Requires rainyhearts font (http://www.dafont.com/rainyhearts.font) and serif font (http://www.dafont.com/7-12-serif.font), or else replace with your own desired font.

Credit to neiio for this being inspired by the Void theme.

Currently only styles the nav bar and does not touch the tab bar. Made for personal use, though any comments/bug reports are welcome.
