### Description
Got tired of new <i>all white</i> twitter and other new changes?
? <b>Bring back animated "Favorite" button</b>.
► <b>Bring back your twitter background</b>.
► <b>Hide "Who to follow" forever</b>.
► <b>Hide "Moments" tab</b>.
► <b>Disable "While you were away" feature</b>.
► <b>Disable "Profile card" on avatar hover</b>.
► <b>Tahoma<sup>®</sup> for Farsi and Arabic tweets</b>.
Any suggestions? <a href="https://twitter.com/arminophen">Share it with me here</a>.
