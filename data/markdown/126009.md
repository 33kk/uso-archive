### Description
Simplenote Minimalismo makes it nicer to use Simplenote by removing distractions and providing a cleaner appearance.
<br /><br /><br />

### Update notes
<b>Features</b>
<ul>
<li>A simpler and cleaner appearance.</li>
<li>Top navigation and notes list are hidden but reappear when you hover your mouse over them.</li>
<li>Centered text area.</li>
<li>Removed footer.</li>
<li>Minimal scrollbar (Chrome only).</li>
</ul>
