### Description
A Google+ Dark Theme derived from Rintan's theme here: https://userstyles.org/styles/153456/dark-google-plus-theme

I've updated it to darken the search bar on the home page and on the communities page, darken buttons and add contrast on them to make them readable, and darkened the whole new post box and collections selector.
