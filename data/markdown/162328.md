### Description
Use this style to blur a tab when it loses mouse focus.

Good for stopping people from reading over your shoulder

### Update notes
<p>Simply add <code>?blur_tab=true</code> to the url parameters on your bookmarks.</p>

<p>If your bookmark already has a '?' in it then you will needs to specify <code>blur_tab=true</code> as an extra argument by placing a '&' before it like <code>www.example.com?foo=bar&blur_tab=true</code></p>
