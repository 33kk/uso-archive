### Update notes
* Font-family used: Georgia, "URW Bookman L", serif.
* Works on Windows, Mac, Linux.
* Applies for all text in page.

* Alternative font-family using [Palatino, "Palatino Linotype", "URW Palladio L", serif]
* Guide used to choose the right font-family: http://www.onedesigns.com/tutorials/font-families-for-cross-compatible-typography
