### Description
Dark theme for MiniCRM.

What is MiniCRM?

A complete sales system that automatizes the sales process professionally, prioritizes leads, manages customer relationships and the processing of customers’ problems. All recorded data is displayed real-time to every user, and the whole customer history is recorded.

It is a cloud-based software, you don’t have to install anything, only need a browser to use it. You can tailor the system to your needs and challenges without the help of an IT manager. It is user-friendly and easy-to-learn – we help you with tips and documentation. Integrate it with your already used systems.
