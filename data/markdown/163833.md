### Description
Clean and elegant design with dark controls and a light content background. Customizable highlight color and option to have Player controls in the sidebar.

<b>Features</b>: 
- Your choice of highlight color<sup>1</sup>
- Option to integrate the player controls into the side bar <sup>2</sup>
- Custom artwork effects, sane font sizes, sensible & responsive layout.

<sup>1</sup> choose one that works on both bright and dark backgrounds
<sup>2</sup> Manually advance a track once to get high resolution artwork to load thereafter.

<b>Suggested Colors</b>: Red: #d92727; Blue: #099fcf; Green: #0bb347; Purple: #8208d3; Gold: #dc9000

Suggestions/Feedback welcome.
