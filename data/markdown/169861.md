### Description
Sets text spacing attributes for accessibility testing <a href="https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG21/#text-spacing">WCAG 2.1 AA 1.4.12</a>
<ul>
<li>Line height (line spacing) to at least 1.5 times the font size;
<li>Spacing following paragraphs to at least 2 times the font size;
<li>Letter spacing (tracking) to at least 0.12 times the font size;
<li>Word spacing to at least 0.16 times the font size.
</ul>
