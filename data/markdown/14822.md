### Description
Removes certain annoyances from the "new-improved" eBay Discussion Boards...  On the "Recent Topics" pages, removes both the left and right columns, disables the balloons from the topic titles, removes the author image content, increases the font size for the topic titles for quick scanning...  On the topic pages (threads), removes the right column, removes the author images associated with the individual posts.
