### Description
More <strong>effective use of space</strong> on bigger screens (e.g. width 1600 pixel) while keeping the standard layout. More width for left and right side bar, bigger chat windows, and removing unneccessary elements. <strong>Customizable before installation.</strong>
Not recommended for usage on small screens.
