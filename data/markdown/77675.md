### Description
With the removal of custom backround images in November 2012 I made this small userstyle. 

Please customize the background-image URL to your needs and add your own local Google domain to it, if it isn't available.
You can use local files too like this: file:///C:/Users/Yourname/Pictures/file.jpg

Example images are taken from Wikimedia Commons, there are a lot more great images that you can use: http://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Widescreen_desktop_backgrounds
