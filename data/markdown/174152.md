### Description
Restores the look of default thumbnails (featuring Snoo) and expansion buttons on old.reddit.com to the way it was prior to the Reddit UI change of 09/2016. This is a re-upload of a now-deleted script made by a redditor. Note: Default Expansion buttons fix only works on YouTube links and text posts.
