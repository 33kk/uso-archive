### Description
A dark grey theme for ResetEra with purple accents. Based on the excellent "ResetEra Dark Theme" by Remy Perona (https://userstyles.org/styles/150164/resetera-dark-theme), modified with permission. Credit to Remy for the original theme which is the base of this theme.

### Update notes
Features:

- Dark theme
- Auto-expands all quotes, no more click to expand
- YouTube videos in quotes are the same size as videos in original posts
- Post area widened to use more screen real estate for better readability
- Your own quoted posts show highlighted in green
- When visiting a previously read thread, your first unread post is highlighted in blue
- Layout tweaks (centered reply/view numbers, pinned icons, etc)
