A dark style I just made for xat that doesn't have some ugly color scheme. Nope. Just a plain, good 'ol dark theme. I don't use this site anymore (I won't judge anybody that does, though) but I thought it would be nice to have a dark theme for it, since you have a dark theme for just about everything these days. Everything is either black or has a dark woods background with white text. Should be easy on the eyes.
