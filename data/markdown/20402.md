### Description
Pale cyan on white? What the hell were they thinking!?

This style makes all link text on Sourceforge.net bold and darker. Unvisited and new links are darkblue. Visited links darkred. Hover is orange.

Paragraph text is black rather than pointlessly-decreased-contrast-gray.
