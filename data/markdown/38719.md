### Description
Type your email address into the "Sign in with your Google Account" area. Hit tab on your keyboard and instead of finding the cursor and focus in the Password field you're inexplicably on a live link to pat@example.com - lame. Install this style to eliminate pat@example.com. Enter your email address. Click tab. Hit enter. You're logged into Google.
