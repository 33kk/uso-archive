### Description
Replaces Firefox 3's Downloads Window appearance to look Like Firefox 2.

Features:
Search Bar is on the Left (Firefox 3 has it on the right)
Clear Button is moved to the Right (on Firefox3 it is on the left) and (on Firefox 2 it was on the right)

Restores the look of the Downloads List by Switching the bottom toolbar's contents.

Browser Versions:
- Works only on Firefox 3
