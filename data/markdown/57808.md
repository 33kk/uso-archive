### Description
Good for e-books in text or html format, or any web site with long articles or hard-to-read text. Standard size sans-serif font. It's not white on black, but light gray text on very dark gray background. I prefer light text on a dark background, but I realize most people prefer the opposite - my style 57807 has dark text on a gray background.
