### Description
This adds the text of the ToolbarButtons 'Back' toward the right. 

To activate it is necessary to change the buttons of the toolbar, to the state of 'only icons'. 

Method: On the toolbar, make a click with the right button (this action opens the contextual menu) and to choose 'Customize'. Then in the new window, check the option 'only icons.'

Compatibility: Firefox 3
