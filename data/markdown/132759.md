### Description
<p><b>Keyhole metaphor</b> in action! My display has 1600 pixels in a row, but git-scm.com shows text in a narrow column, wastes space for useless margins.
<p>Let's utilize <i>our</i> displays! Let's kill this stupid fixed-width design!
<p>This style removes most of the borders and margins leaving maximum space for the content. Also, it resets all the font settings, so you will see the page in <i>your</i> favorite font.
<p>Enjoy,
VdB
