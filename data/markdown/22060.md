### Description
With this userstyle you can remove the ad bar at the top of game pages on FunOrb. Removed will be the ad bar and "Logged in as", and you are left with the navigation links Home, Game List, and Switch Server.

This style also removes any text directly below the game (usually used to help diagnose whitescreen problems).
