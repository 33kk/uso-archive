### Update notes
<strong>Below are a list of the variables and what they do.</strong>

Home Button Image - This will change the picture of the "CREC" on the top left.

Background Image Overlay - This is an image that will be repeated on the background.

Background Color - Changes the background to go with a transparent image.

Background Transparency - The transparency of the white frames and everything in them.

TopBar Color - The color of the green top bar.

TopBar Transparency - The transparency of the top bar.

Lock Topbar - Locks the top bar to the top of your screen.
