Instructions on how to apply the cursors
========================================
1. Go to this site http://www.cursors-4u.com/anime
2. Choose your cursor and click its link
3. On the "Cursor Download And Information", you will see a download button. Instead of saving it, copy the cursor URL(For Google: Right click + Copy link address).
4. Paste the URL of the cursor below and click "+ Install with Stylish" and your done :3
