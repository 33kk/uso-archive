### Description
Adblocker for dagbladet.no and kjendis.no

The ads are simply removed, and will in some places leave empty gaps. There is no repositioning of any content or changes to the design. I have written a version that takes care of some of the gaps and also allows full customization of the right sidebar. It can be found here: style 1999.
