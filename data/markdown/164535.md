### Description
Google Calendar Colorize Weekend on Month View

Using itam.jp's Colorize Google Calendar Weekend as base point, this style colorize weekends (last 2 days of week) on month view and other views. 

This style works with such locale when weeks are starting on Monday (as Spain locale).
