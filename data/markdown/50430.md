### Description
This script removes the search bar on top and makes the leftside toolbar autohide. Move your mouse to the left side of the browser to make it reappear. The goal is to maximize screenspace without losing functionality.

Compatible with the September 2011 google calendar interface changes.

Based on "Google Calendar -- Full Screen Calendar": http://userstyles.org/styles/50260/google-calendar-full-screen-calendar
