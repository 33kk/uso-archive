### Description
Themes the Custom Persona Editor by displaying a faded transparent Mozilla Personas logo.
Also changes the background of the page to the background color found on the Personas Gallery webpage

Features:
- The Personas logo will display transparently on the page; behind the name, header, and footer selections.
- The page background color is changed to the color found on getpersonas.com/gallery
- Fully customizable style, for your own enjoyment.

Instructions:
You may also change the background HEX color or image to your liking.
