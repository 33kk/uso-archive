### Description
Stephano is a character created by PewDiePie. He is a golden-tanned statue from the Amnesia series, usually found by Pwedie in a bookcase or near a barrel. Stephano is a fearless Frenchmen with a small beard and a sword. When Pewdie is scared or distracted, Stephano advises him to not make noises or doing certain actions that will alarm enemies.
