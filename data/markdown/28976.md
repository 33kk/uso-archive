### Description
Libero (http://liberomail.libero.it/) is probably the largest e-mail provider in Italy. 

Libero Mail comes in two flavors:
[*] Standard - Slow, ugly layout.
[*] Beta - Simple layout, good performance.

This style is aimed to clean the Beta version from all those intrusive and annoying banners, way easy than Adblock Plus.
