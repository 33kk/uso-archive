### Description
This creates a 300 px wide Google map (fully searchable and dragable) with a starting location of your choice, perfect width for the sidebar.

To use it:

1. DO NOT APPLY THE STYLE!  Create your starting position by plugging in an address here:

 http://www.sfgate.com/cgi-bin/maps/google/map

2. Bookmark the resulting link and apply the style.  Notice how the page shrinks to 300 px.

3. Right click on the bookmark and select Properties, check the "Load this bookmark in the sidebar" box.

You may need to change the height of the map (.mapsize) to fit your browser's vertical resolution.
