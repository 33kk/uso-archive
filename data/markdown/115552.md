### Description
Ever wished the Tumblr Dashboard didn't have the horizontal scrollbar when everything clearly fit on the screen? With Responsive Dashboard, that wish has come true! 

Features:
<ul>
<li>Hides the horizontal scrollbar unless it's really needed.</li>
<li>Slims the margins on many Tumblr pages such as the Dashboard.</li>
<li>Makes the Dashboard responsive by automatically hiding the sidebar if the window gets too small for it.</li>
<li>Reduces the width of the header to match the Dashboard.</li>
<li>Expands the search box to fill the header's available space.</li>
</ul>
