### Description
White style for Fox.com (Fox Broadcasting Company).  This style makes readability very easy with vista's Calibri font, as well as the white styling.

Style covers the following websites (all children domains of fox.com):
- 24
- Terminator: The Sarah Connor Chronicles
- American Dad
- Are You Smarter Than a 5th Grader
- Back to You
- Bones
- Hell's Kitchen
- House
- Bones
- King of the Hill
- New Amsterdam
- Spike Fertesen
- Till Death
- Prison Break
...
- Countless Others


Features:
- Calibri font (Supports Windows XP and Windows 9x by including "Arial" as
the secondary Font-Family if Calibri is not found.)
- White background
- White tables
- Page navigation menus are black background / white text
- Yellow and Orange theme for 24
- Bright / light overall look, much like a newspaper
- Cleanup of any Flash Ads that don't load
