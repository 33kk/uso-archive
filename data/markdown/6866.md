### Description
Removes the ads from the Shaw.ca Email (Webmail) Login page (Shaw's Java Web Mail 2.0). This drastically improves pageload times and allows you to log on and read your emails faster.

Features:
- Removes the advertising image on the welcome / logon screen
- No longer do you have to wait until the page loads completely before you begin logging in
