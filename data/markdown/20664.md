### Description
The new design of PWInsider.com is nice, but it could be a little nicer. All I did was shift all of the content to the left side, shift all of the sidebar ads to the right side, and move some ads down the page. I did not choose to remove any advertisements, if you want to block ads on the site, then please use an appropriate tool such as AdBlock Plus.

This ONLY works on PWInsider.com, and not other related sites such as PWI Extra or PWI Elite.
