### Description
A quick and dirty YouTube comment section hider, because, who wants to read those?
To show comments simply disable the style, enable to hide again.
(Hides normal comment section and live broadcast comment feed)

Does not work with AlienTube - an intended and unintended side effect. To turn off comments with AlienTube use AlienTube settings.
