### Description
Collapses all of the additional tweet information: quotes, media, Twitter cards. Hover over the tweet's media tag to reveal the collapsed content. Optionally hide promotional tweets. Only affects tweets in your stream, not tweets viewed individually.

### Update notes
2017-10-32 - Collapsed content is only revealed when hovering over the media tags, not the entire tweet.
2017-07-27 - Fixed media tagging positioning.
2017-06-19 - Initial version.
