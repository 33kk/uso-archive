### Description
This theme tries to make the new Twitter design look less different from its previous design.The top bar is brought back and colors of things are made to match the older twitter. There is support for all default colour schemes.

If you like it, please leave a positive rating.

Contact me on  <a href="https://twitter.com/TritiumDecay">My Twitter</a> if you wish to suggest a change.
