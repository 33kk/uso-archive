### Description
Productive People use Youtube for learning, inspiration, sometimes for leisure in order to take a break and relax. They don't get caught up in the useless neverending entertaining non-beneficial content.

This style: 
- Remove the Trending Button from the Menu
- Remove the Home Button from the Menu
- Remove the comments section on a video
- Remove the "Watch Next" section on a video
- Remove the "Home" content
