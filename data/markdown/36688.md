For Firefox 3.6+ and up to the latest Firefox Beta, Aurora, Nightly builds.

Hides Firefox Sync in the Firefox "Options Window" and its old Status-bar Icon on Firefox 3.6(Also hides "Sync Links" on Fx4+ about:home)
