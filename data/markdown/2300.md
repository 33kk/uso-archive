### Description
With Adblock Plus 0.7.5 the list of blockable items moved to the bottom of the window. This style will move it to the left side again, by removing one of the marked lines you can also have it on the right or on the top.

Thanks to Malte Kraus aka Dr.Evil for coming up with this.
