### Description
Add goals to Google Calendar, reminds you to schedule your priorities, instead of prioritizing your schedule.

### Update notes
You have three sections to edit: 
1. The main title (replacing the word "Calendar" with whatever your primary goal is for the month or year. Current example is "Bourbon")
2. The month (append your goal after the month - current example is "- Monthly Goal"
3. The sidebar (add a few goals between the mini-calendar and your list of calendars; current example is "Side Goal1, Side Goal 2")
