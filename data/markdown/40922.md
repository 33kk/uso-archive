### Description
Cleaner version of the popular task management app Remember The Milk. Main benefit is in moving the lists tabs to a more readable format as a sidebar on the left.

Features:
-moved lists to left sidebar
-lengthened search bar
-removed logo
-removed task cloud
-removed the "complete" and "postpone" buttons (use the shortcuts!)
-slight change to style of flash notices
-footer styled to be less prominent
