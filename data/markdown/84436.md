### Description
WARNING: this doesn't work with the new facebook top bar. i made a style compatible with the new facebook top bar here: http://userstyles.org/styles/98838/new-white-minimal-facebook

a clean, white, minimal facebook for your viewing pleasure. this theme neatens up your top bar and makes it white, hides ads, and reduces sidebar clutter.

NOTE: it hides all the sidebars except favourites, so move all your necessary groups and apps up there for easy access.
