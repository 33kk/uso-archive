### Description
<b>NOTE: As of 01.01.2013 this style will no longer be supported.</b>
This style displays the time/date the post was posted while hovering over the message body. When hovering the time/date stamp, the text will highlight, allowing you to click through to follow the link to the actual post. The dog-eared corner has been removed and a text highlight added to indicate a view page hover. The hover area has been extended to the middle of the top of each post. Enjoy!
