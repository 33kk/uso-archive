### Description
Enjoy the depth of the game playing in the depth of the deep blue ocean!
Advance your prawns to attack the enemy! Protect the octopus king by castling with the blowfish!
Use the seahorse and the Calamari and perform an offensive fried liver attack! Or should i say fried calamari? And be aware not to lose the jellyfish!
