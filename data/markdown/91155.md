### Description
http://sbcx.saic.gov.cn/trade-e/ , also known as China Trademark Website, is the official search engine for trademark registration information, which is maintained by the Trademark Office of China. For unknown reasons, all registered graphic trademarks are mirror-inverted in that site (as shown in the screenshots). sbcx.saic.gov.cn uses fliter:flipV to rectify the images. However, that method never works in Chrome, Firefox or Safari, and is no longer supported by IE9 or later.

http://sbcx.saic.gov.cn/trade/ ，即中国商标网，是中国商标局维护的用于查询商标注册信息的官方搜索引擎。由于未知原因，该网站的所有注册的图形商标均被镜像倒置（如截图所示）。sbcx.saic.gov.cn使用flipV滤镜对图像进行了纠正。但是，该方法一直不能用于Chrome，Firefox或Safari，而且不再为IE9或更高版本支持。
