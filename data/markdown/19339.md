### Description
This is for all the insomniacs on reddit. It dims reddit.com down for night reading. Images are dimmed down until you put your mouse over them. It works well with the new changes to reddit. For a better experience make sure you uncheck "allow reddits to show me custom styles" under the "display options" in your reddit "preferences." Hope you like the theme my fellow redditors.
