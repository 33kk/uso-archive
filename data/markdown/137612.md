### Description
Removes Wikia advertisements and advertisement-like "navigation" (links to other Wikia wikis and Fandom articles etc.) Does not remove sidebar content relevant to the wiki you're on.

You may also wish to check out style 137613, style 137367, and style 162390, which I've tried to keep compatible. Combine them to make Wikia look how you want!
