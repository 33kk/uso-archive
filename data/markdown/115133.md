### Description
This tumblr dashboard theme has background options for every color of the rainbow and comes in two versions: a space version and a supernova version. It includes gently animated post icons for every corresponding background color, four home buttons and four tumblr logo fonts. All color combinations feature a fade-as-you-scroll black transparent content box, for anyone who still has their right column options (not shown). <b>If you want something adjusted, customized, removed, or have any other questions or concerns, feel free to contact me <a href="http://www.eatyourheirs.tumblr.com/ask">@eatyourheirs</a>.</b>
