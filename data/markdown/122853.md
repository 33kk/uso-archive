### Description
This style changes the theme <a href=https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/simplewhitex/><b><i>Simple White X</i></b></a> for <b>Firefox</b>.  It makes the toolbars have a light gray background similar to the background in <b>Firefox</b>'s <b><i>default</i></b> theme.  It darkens the frame area (with the section tabs) of the <i>Options</i> and the <i>Add-ons Manager</i> pages. It also darkens the background of the panels from the buttons in the toolbar, e.g., the menu. (Updated for <b>2.2b18.x1.09</b>.)
