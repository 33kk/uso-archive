### Description
"Compact Bookmarks Toolbar" for Firefox squeezes the space between each item on the Bookmarks Toolbar and removes their icons.
It allows you to store more items on the Bookmarks Toolbar (before they appear in the dropdown list on the end).

Tested with FF1.5.0.6 and FF2.0b2.
