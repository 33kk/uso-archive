### Description
Many colleges use D2L Brightspace as a hub to give assignments, grades, notes, etc. I think the site looks ugly, but not anymore! I did some redesigning to make it prettier and in line with Google's Material Design spec.

### Update notes
The selectors might take some tweaking if your college has a different version.
If you want the most Material experience, use colors from <a href="http://www.google.com/design/spec/style/color.html#color-color-palette">Google's color palette</a>.
