### Description
Webmasters, bored by your habitual Adsense Console (where you can see all the stats, manage your ads, etc) ? Change its design ! 
For more visibility of statistics in the tables, a border for each td has been added, plus a new dark background more clear and restful, and some new things such as hovers. 

This style is under construction, so if you have any suggestion please don't hesitate.
