### Description
<b><i>It's ONLY for a WIDESCREEN(1920x1080)!</i></b>
<b>► The Goal: use at its maximum my WideScreen:</b>
- for the shots (Maximize the number of shots visible in the window)
- for the Info (scroll list and hover)
- for an admin happy life (adaptation for selected Greasemonkey scripts)
- Less scrolling with a compact and useful styled interface.
- Less "IN THE FACE" style (Smaller version of the shots in Photo Page and Justified Views).

►► <b>What This Userstyle Change ? :</b>
========================================
Some special advantages are offer by this Userstyle:

► <b>Hidden Flickr Nav bar</b> : Hide the Top Nav Bar until you hover the Litlle Red Arrow ( at the middle/ top).
or the MAIL Alert Envelope Icon with the number of Mails Waiting (Appear only if you receive a mail...)

► <b>Alternative to the Justified  in the ARCHIVES pages</b>: Show the Hidden Options Bar.
So, Now you can chose the ARCHIVES Thumbnail size (Square -  Small - Madium - Large)

► <b>Thumbnail Zoom Plus facility</b>: Always maintain the capacity to use <a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/fr/firefox/addon/thumbnail-zoom-plus/">Thumbnail Zoom Plus, Firefox Addon</a> and see the larger Photo. 

► <b>All "too flashy" Generics Buddy User Icons are muted </b>: Styled to be gray by a grayscale filter

►► <b>PHOTO Page Very Clear</b> The Place for the photo is big (Heavy Use of Hover...): 
All is visible in the windows without scrolling.
Infos / Actions are  moved on Right Side of the Photo:
- Left Side/ 2/3 space for the Photo or Video
- Right Side: 1/3 space for Infos and Actions.

- <b>PHOTO Title on Top, Description stay at the bottom</b> (hidden until you hover an arrow)
- <b>Comment Form </b> moved Right Side, Near User Avatar.
- Comments are hidden and visible until you hover the "Comment Icon" near the Comment Form.
They are shown by an overlay on the picture.  A Comment counter is added to know where you are... .
- <b>Polls / SETs / Favs List</b>: Counter, Scrollable , and compacted (Use Hover if too long).
- <b>Panels "People in this Photo", "Tags" and "About  Flickr": Moved at the bottom of the page</b> (visible on hover too).


<b>►► Others Pages:</b>
