Setting your own <b>BACKGROUND:</b>
Since <b>v0.5.5</b> it is possible to set your own background for <b>ANY</b> color scheme my Theme provides.
<b><i>If you do not want to change your background and use the default provided by the color scheme, set this field to <u>default</u>!</i></b>
The format for this field is: <b><code>url(your_image_url)!important</code></b>
The only thing you should replace in there it the <b>your_image_url</b>, but make it a short one, since UserStyles.org limits this field to 50 characters, so reupload your images to www.imgur.com or any image hosting service that can give you short links.
In case you want your image stretchend on the full background of the page, you can try a suggestion posted on the discussion forums https://forum.userstyles.org/discussion/54224/body-image-suggestion
