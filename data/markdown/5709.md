### Description
QQ Bookmarking is very pretty but del.icio.us is simple. You can enter http://shuqian.qq.com/popular/ to have a taste. The style made reference to its appearance. Thanks to Tencent Inc.
Also the style makes page links created by Pagerization script pretty.
Notice that the style is only semi finished!!! It mainly supports personal bookmarks page such as http://del.icio.us/USERNAME and tag searching page such as http://del.icio.us/tag/TAGNAME.

QQ书签的界面比较漂亮，这个样式表就是参考了它的界面，你可以打开http://shuqian.qq.com/popular/体验一下。谢谢腾讯。
如果你使用Pagerization自动翻页脚本，它产生的简洁的页面链接也会变漂亮。
注意：这个脚本只是一个半成品，暂时主要支持个人书签页面(地址形如http://del.icio.us/用户名)和标签搜索页面(地址形如http://del.icio.us/tag/标签名)，以后是否会支持其他页面不敢保证。
