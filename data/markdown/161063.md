### Description
Minor style tweaks that improve the display of Brainscape Flash Cards for the Thai language (both Thai script and phonetic pronunciation systems)

- Sets font-family to "Arial Unicode MS"
- Makes flash card text larger and centre-aligned
- Hides the Help button (which fouls the right-most score button)
