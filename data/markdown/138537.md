### Description
Customize the scroll bar's colors and thickness (useful for touch screens)

### Update notes
Use this style to modify Chrome's scroll bar. Especially useful on touch screen laptops, where the scrollbar is too small/thin to scroll easily. But useful for anybody.

Defaults to a scrollbar 30px thick and a dark color theme. But you can customize it with your own desired width and color scheme.
