### Description
Adoc is a free tool provided by the FFT (French federation of tennis) to ease the administration of a tennis club and associations in France. Adoc has been redesigned in 2018, July the 25th with a complete new template. This last template is not contrasted enough, and then not adapted to every person, depending on the vision accuracy of the person.
