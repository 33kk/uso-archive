### Description
A dark theme for Firefox Quantum 57+. Inspired by the matte charcoal Lamborghini Aventador LP700. I love the 3D effect the subtle gradient delivers over the flat design of the other new FF Quantum themes I've seen. Copy CSS to a blank userChrome.css file and enjoy!
