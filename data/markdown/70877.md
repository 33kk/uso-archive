### Description

Matthias

### Update notes
Notes:
- You may want to disable stacking of pages and have pages aligned as usual. By default, stacking is enabled 
  (see style options above).
- You may have to adapt Speed Dial's background browser and preview settings if the preview images do not fit 
  the windows size (don't forget to reload the page preview afterwards).
- If you like to see your favorite background, just add an empty first row.
