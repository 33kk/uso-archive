### Description
Changes the YouTube Channel design ("One channel", beta as of March 2013), to make it look a lot more like the older design. Works with auto-generated topics, too. (Hides related channels and guide, centres the channel, old channel colours and more). Click for more information, feedback is much appreciated!

List of changes:
---------------
* Works with channels, playlists and auto-generated content
* Hides related channels and guide
* Centres the channel
* Old channel design (old colours and patterns)
* Old channel avatar display
* Old subscribers count
* Old related channels style
* More/Less Video columns (Number of videos shown)

The CSS is fully commented and almost all changes are optional, so edit, remove and choose to your choice.
