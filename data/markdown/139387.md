### Description
Disables the stupid, terrible, obnoxious pop-out video player that is currently plaguing cnet.com. 

<b>NOTE: Unfortunately, a CSS style can't keep the video from autoplaying, but you can use the "Disable HTML5 Autoplay" Chrome extension to keep the video from playing.</b>
