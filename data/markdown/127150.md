### Description
Allows to revert the TweetDeck' tweet footer rearrangement from late April 2016.

### Update notes
Provided are options to either completely revert the change, or keep the semi-transparent buttons but move them to the right and style the "view conversation" link to fit with buttons when the tweet is not being hovered upon.
