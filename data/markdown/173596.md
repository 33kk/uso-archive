### Description
Some small tweaks for Twitter's new 2019 layout (dim / dark mode)...Please ensure dim / dark mode is turned on when using. (Should be) compatible with any colour chosen from the site's display options.

- Removes Twitter logo in top left corner (same link as Home button)
- Makes bold font less bold/thick
- Dims left sidebar font colours
- Makes tweets/feed different colour than background colour

This was done on a whim and probably won't be updated, feel free to modify/tweak for personal use.
