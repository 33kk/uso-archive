### Description
Zoho Mail's Cobalt Blue dark theme enhanced for better readability.

- Wider and compact folder panel.
- Split the Subject and the message starting part into two lines.
- Applied subtle light background for read mails.
- Wider Preview panel.
- Stream section on the folder panel hidden.
