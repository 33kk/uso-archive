### Description
A skin to make the old reader look more like the google reader:
- Fixed width posts so it is easier to read on wider screen resolutions
- read posts have different styling than unread
- indented subscriptions in the menu to easily distingush subscription group headers from the subscription names
- color scheme similar to google reader's one
