### Description
Disable several items that overlay YouTube videos.

<ul>
<li><strong>Annotations:</strong> you know what these are, because the option to turn them off never seems to stay OFF!.</li>
<li><strong>Cards:</strong> this button and teaser that appears in the upper right corner of videos and displays as a lowercase `i` in a circle.</li>
<li><strong>Cross Suggestions:</strong> These are the end card overlays that appear during the last few seconds of the video that ask for your subscription or suggest other video.</li>
</ul>
