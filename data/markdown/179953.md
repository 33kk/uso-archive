### Description
Use YouTube to keep up with your subscriptions only, not get distracted by other videos.

Entirely removes home screen content. Removes recommendations from every video. Removes links to "Trending" and "Originals" pages.

You should probably turn off autoplay before enabling this style, as you won't be able to see what's up next.
