### Description
Squaring off all new wide format photos and videos for old school vibe. Multi-column feed, bigger photos on profile pages, bigger post pages and post overlays

### Update notes
Vertical oriented photos are not squared in feed

To view full pictures and like and/or comment open posts in the new tab or disable the style. Right-clicking on the images will open full size pictures as style doesn't actually alter any files.
