### Description
Hide swf from MyMiniCity pages to shorten the load time.
City image and ads are hidden.
City name and local ranking are displayed.
In addition, news bulletin is larger than original, and some background images are hidden.

If you think this style is useful, please visit my city: utoland.myminicity.com

update May 6, 2008.
Comments are added in CSS.
Level descriptions are also hidden to easier access to xml button.
