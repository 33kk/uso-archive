### Description
This is a dark-themed, subdued style for Craigslist. It maintains much of Craigslist's simplistic design, however modernizes the aesthetics of it.

### Update notes
Version 1.3

By default, this theme is mostly chromatic, however you may pick different colors for various parts of the site. Colors I have found go well with it are:

Dark olive: #3A4006
subdued blue: #1B344D
bleached red: #4D1C24
