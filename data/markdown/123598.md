### Description
You don't know what «<a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/advanced-locationbar/"><strong>Advanced Locationbar</strong></a>» (ex. <i>Locationbar²</i>) means?
Tis a great add-on to style the location bar within your Firefox.

And here is some CSS to make it look even nicer... at least, this is my opinion! ;)

There are different colors for different document types.
Currently there are 3 different «colored» groups:
• Static documents (<code>.htm</code>, <code>.html</code> and <code>.txt</code>)
• Dynamic documents (<code>.php</code>, <code>.php3</code>, <code>.php4</code>, <code>.cgi</code>, <code>.asp</code>, <code>.jsp</code>, <code>.cfm</code>)
• Images (<code>.jpeg</code>, <code>.jpg</code>, <code>.bmp</code>, <code>.tiff</code>, <code>.png</code>, <code>.gif</strong>)
