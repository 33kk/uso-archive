### Description
When I logged into AU Access for the first time, I didn't find the visual style appealing at all. Less than seven hours later, I wrote the first version of this style. It should iron out many of the kinks in the style, and make it much prettier and maybe even easier to use.

I hope to keep this updated as long as I have an AU access account, so at least the next four years. If you want a specific page fixed, comment on this style.
