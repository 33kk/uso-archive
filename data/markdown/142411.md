### Description
A dark theme for Tapas which was previously known as Tapastic.

Only the color palettes were changed without modifying the overall design of the site. 

This theme is also good for using in a dark environment to prevent the melting of the eye balls, or if you just prefer a darker look for the site.


If there are any problems, please leave a comment. I will try to fix it whenever I can.
