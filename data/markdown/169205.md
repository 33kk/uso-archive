### Description
<strong>Hey! 👋🏽</strong>

Darklingo++ is a delightful switch on the default Duolingo UI. This style presents you with a <strong>Dark Mode</strong>, the ability to change the <strong>Theme Color</strong> , and it makes both leagues and the scrollbar optional. <i>(More to come in the future)</i>

If you find anything that isn't themed correctly, feel free to tell me; feedback is always welcome! 😁 

And if you <i>really</i> like this style or any of my others, you can <a href="https://paypal.me/justinikeako">donate</a> (only if you want to 🤗).
