<mark>--Options--</mark>

<b>Add ESGST Compatibility (default OFF)</b>
Modifies the base style for ESGST compatibility.

<b>Reveal Spoiler Text (default ON)</b>
Makes spoiler text readable without the need of hovering the mouse cursor (it doesn't completely reveal spoilers so you know when there's something hidden).

<b>Hide Patreon Cosmetics (default ON)</b>
Removes cosmetic extras from profiles and names.

<b>Insert Background Image (default ON)</b>
Adds a low opacity image to the background.

<b>Background URL</b>
Pick a default image, or upload your favorite.
Recommended settings:
- your screen resolution (ie: 1920x1080)
- JPG quality: low (the image is blurred so it doesn't matter)

<b>Background Blur (default MEDIUM)</b>
Applies a blur effect to the background to make elements more prominent.

<b>Reveal Hidden Links (default ON)</b>
Makes hidden links visible without the need to check the source code. Limited detection in Chrome, full in Firefox.

<b>Mark Visited Links (default ON)</b>
Changes the color of visited links in comments (discussions, giveaways, tickets) for easier tracking.
