### Description
Usability improvements for wide monitors (1920+ pixels)
<ul>
<li>Wide search box</li>
<li>Compact horizontal user menu</li>
<li>Compact footer</li>
<li>Fixed thumbnail size (size will no longer adjust to the window width, making them bigger at the cost of some white space on the right)</li>
<li>Post rating borders (border colors will change depending on image tags, similar to FurAffinity)</li>
<li>Fixed download and pagination buttons position</li>
</ul>
