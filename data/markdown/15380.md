### Description
The sidebar is located under the Tabbar.
When the sidebar is opened, it prevents that the tabbar shrinks. 

This style is based on "Firefox 3 Tabs at the Top" By Sabeur.

[Known Issue]
In fullscreen mode, empty box is remained at the top of the sidebar.
