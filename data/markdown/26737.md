### Description
Under Linux (and maybe other operating systems too?), when a Persona is in use, the mouseover highlight on toolbar buttons is replaced with a simple rectangle.  This style restores the system look-and-feel for toolbar buttons when a Persona is being used.

Tested on 3.5.8 with version 1.5.2 of the Personas extension.
