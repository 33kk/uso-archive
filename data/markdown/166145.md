### Description
A clean dark-theme for www.racefans.net, featuring:

&bullet; Darkened feed, articles, comment-sections, photo-galleries and forum.
&bullet; Removal of layout-clutter to optimize readability.
&bullet; Adjusted font-sizes, margin and padding to optimize content-width within the given design.
&bullet; Tweaked the rather primitively main-menubar design.
&bullet; Added some box-shadowing, curved borders and other subtle CSS changes to give it a more modern look.
&bullet; Suppressed most - intrusively annoying - ads (full width header, footer and inline in article blocks).
