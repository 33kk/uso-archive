### Description
[ FEATURES ]
On image-only pages:
	- Images are centered, both horizontally and vertically.
	- A border appears when cursor touches image. (Not on SVG images)
	- Transparency-tiles appear when cursor touches PNG, GIF, or ICO images with transparent areas.
	
Compatible with local image-files viewed in browser.
