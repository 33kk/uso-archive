### Description
Twitter.com started showing images, videos, cards, and sales forms inline in your feed. There's no option to turn it off. This style restores the old click-to-expand behavior for tweeted images, videos, and cards. It also unhides now-invisible media links (e.g. pic.twitter.com).

Quoted tweets are displayed inline, but quoted images are hidden. Click the parent tweet to expand and reveal the quoted image.
