### Description
Script fixing forum presentation for low to medium screen resolution. On large resolution displays it can make it worse.

<br><br>
- Fix large resolution image placement. <br>
- Fix pre tag size.<br>
- Reduce left (user data) part of  post to free more space to right/informative part.<br>
- When post with images/youtube quoted (referenced) in other post, images/youtube in quote reduced in size. But you can expand images to 100% by moving mouse over.<br>
