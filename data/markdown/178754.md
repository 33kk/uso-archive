### Description
<b>Dribbble Dark Mode</b>
Dribbble Dark Mode gives Dribbble a darker look for a better viewing experience and makes it easier to focus on the shots you're watching.

<b>Features:</b>
- Dark Mode
- Support the brand new dribbble design
- Colorful style theme
- Block Ads
- Stay updated until Dribbble launches the official dark mode.
