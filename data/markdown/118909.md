Widens:
- inventory history 
https://steamcommunity.com/my/inventoryhistory/

- live trade 
https://steamcommunity/trade/x

- lists of offers received and sent 
https://steamcommunity.com/my/tradeoffers

- trade offer response page 
steamcommunity.com/tradeoffer/[trade offer id]
