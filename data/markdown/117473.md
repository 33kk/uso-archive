### Description
Solarized Light Firefox theme using, well, probably Solarized Light colour pallets maybe.

Theme fit to suit you material flat google raping MUH MINIMALISM!! hipster faglords.

1. Any top bar items must be removed for it to look properly. 

2. You can use "The Addon Bar (restored)" (firefox extension) to have your items visible on the bottom without breaking the CSS theme aesthetics. 

3.Use "Customize" to drag any items to a blank area in the "Customize" firefox option.

4. Use that same menu to then drag your items to your addon bar extension

5. "ctrl + /" to toggle view/hide "Addon Bar (restored)" bar.
