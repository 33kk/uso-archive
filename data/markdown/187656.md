### Description
Dracula, Material, Minimal

Persistent table of contents, dark colors, material boxes, minimal linking and noise.

For full effect in Firefox, combine with these two other addons:

https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/dracula-dark-colorscheme/
https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/custom-scrollbars/

To make your scrollbar have the same colors as in the main pic, use:
scrollbar thumb color: #282a36ff
scrollbar track color: #44475aff
