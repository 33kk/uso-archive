### Description
Makes the background of the navigation toolbar become dark when the window is maximized. This style works better after optionally removing the menubar with the 'Hide Menubar' extension (https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/4762). Also works better for Vista Aero.

After implementing this style, the location bar and search bar might look ugly, so try style 3612.
