### Description
This style aims to remove clutter and provide cleaner look for medium theme by in-lining sticky elements in articles.

* Makes header and footer un-sticky
* Minimizes title section
* Adds custom article width (Use Customize Settings option. Default: 1096px)

 Applies to:
* Medium.com
* freecodecamp.org
* hackernoon.com
* codeburst.io
* itnext.io
