### Description
This style makes Yahoo Mail page full-width, and hides most of the annoying crap: ALL "message list" ads, right-side ad panel, Breaking News, Views, Contacts button, Calendar button, Notepad button, and Yahoo Video nonsense in empty folders.

One minor inconvenience: also hides the Settings button. For those few times when you need to access Settings, you can just temporarily disable the style.

Comments & suggestions are welcome.
