### Description
When the night has come, and the land is dark, and the moon is the only light... Keep your eyes healthy with the best facebook dark theme ever!</br>Full Theme (personal profile, business page, chat, messenger, marketplace, events, calendar, video player, stories...)
<b>messenger.com and facebook.onion supported! Social plugins/widgets excluded!</b>
Feel free to tell me if you see any problems, I'll try to fix them quickly!
<b>For colored icons, use the "Customize Settings" button, thanks.</b>
<b>Please turn the new Facebook dark mode on, thanks</b>
