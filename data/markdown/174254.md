### Description
CLICK "CUSTOMIZE SETTINGS" (next to "Install Style")
Choose the Twitter theme you plan to use this with, and whether you want regular or wide-screen feed.

---

Theme for new Twitter PWA 2019 layout. Sets background to light grey for more comfortable viewing and removes Trends. Shrinks the size of the right sidebar. Removes border around feed. Option for a more spacious widescreen feed.

---

To change theme settings after install: Refresh the page, choose new settings, and click "Update Style".
