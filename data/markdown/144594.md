### Description
Folds the header, hovering over the 10px area above the nav will show show it again. 
You can change if you want the folding header on all pages (default) or only on the watch page. 
Tested with a resolution of 1366 x 768 so everything above that should also look good.

Designed for "KissAnime - Project: ABE", but it also works without it.
