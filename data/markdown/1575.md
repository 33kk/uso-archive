### Description
Many people on the EWbattleground.com forum desired smaller text on the orangness skin to make it like the warmer blueness skin. This style makes this so.

And just to make it clear, this style will turn any skin into that size text. It's skin-neutral. It's only labelled as orangness skin because thats the one I use.
