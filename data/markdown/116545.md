### Description
Narrow column and fixed header design is stupid and ridiculous. Let's fuck "keyhole metaphor" and get back to user-friendly "Plain Old Design":

<ol>
<li>No narrow columns, no huge margins.
<li>No horizontal scrolling.
<li>No fixed headers and footers.
</ol>

Let users choose window width, font style and character size.

Enjoy, VdB
