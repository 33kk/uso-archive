### Description
This is my first user style! This is a simple change to Pandora that keeps most everything the same. Basically it just removes the bright stuff from the playing screen and leaves the rest. Menu popups will still be light.

Note: The screenshot shows it being used with Adblock Plus (an extension for Chrome and Firefox). Without Adblock Plus, there will be ads.

Works in Firefox and Chrome.
