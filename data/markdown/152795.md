### Description
Helps you stay focused by preventing enticing video recommends removing suggested videos and promotions from Youtube's pause menu, embeds, overlays and side of the video. 
Optionally: Hide videos from the homescreen or comments.

<b>This script has also become <a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/focused-youtube/">an add-on</b></a>

Highly recommend to use along-side an adblocker like uBlock.

Updated for latest Youtube version and is compatible with most other Youtube mods.
