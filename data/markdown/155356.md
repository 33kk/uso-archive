### Description
Custom theme for Moodle used by the University of Mauritius
Once installed click on Stylish icon and edit the theme to add the following rules:

Click on Specify at the bottom, then:
Add > URLs on the domain name > http://lcms.uom.ac.mu/lms
Add > URLs matching the regexp > lms
