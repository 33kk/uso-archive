### Description
Specifically made for Wattpad.com. It removes the images and youtube videos from the "Multimedia" section on stories.

C'est pour wattpad.com. Il retire les images et les vidéos de youtube de la partie "Multimédia" dans les histoires.

Made for those tired of seeing random site models, everyone from <em>Gossip Girl</em>, the Hemsworth brothers, Bradley Cooper, and another random star "cast" as a character in a book. 

Also does away with distracting photobucket slideshows.
