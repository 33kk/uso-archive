### Description
Recolours the InsaneJournal portal from light peach to dark grey to reduce eyestrain.  Intended for use with New Tweak, IJ's default site theme, but looks fine with most light-coloured themes (like Insanime, Clean, Lynx, Opal Cat, both Worksafes and both Dramatics).
