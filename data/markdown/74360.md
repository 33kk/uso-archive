### Description
I had to go onto Forbes today and was surprised at the state of it. This fixes the ads, the unnecessary bar at the top and the blocky and bolded text on the headings. It also removes the collapsible bar at the bottom, the superfluous share bars and buttons. In the articles, it centers the article, makes the text more legible and a few other small changes.
