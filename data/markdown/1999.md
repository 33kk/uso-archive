### Description
Adblocker for dagbladet.no and kjendis.no

This removes the ads in the same way as style 1996 do. In addition this version removes two big gaps by moving the boxes containing a summary of the latest news and the most commented articles to the right sidebar. It should be quite easy to customize the sidebar to meet your needs by playing around with this code.
