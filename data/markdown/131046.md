### Description
Makes the webcomic "Order of the Stick" easier to read by making the comic centered on page, removing all clutter and making the background a better color.

Get <a href="http://wekateka.com/userscripts/oots_arrow.user.js">this Greasemonkey userscript</a> to be able to use your left/right arrow keys to navigate between comics.
