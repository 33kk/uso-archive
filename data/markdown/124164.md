### Description
A clean white theme for Wikipedia. Inspired by the 1911 theme by rotane and intended to be a free opensource replacement for Wikiwand. It is cleaner and uses serif font to make the content look more academic.

Hover mouse on left for sidebar, hover on right for contents.
