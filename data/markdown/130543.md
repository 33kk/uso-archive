>>>>> <u>Brightness choice :</u>
I recommend a brightness of 90%, but you can choose any value between 0%~100%.
>>>>> <u>Background choice :</u>
I changed the page background to a black one instead of the default white one, but you can choose the picture you want ( /!\ Resolution of the image you want : it may not be fully displayed.)
>>>>> <u>Page Toolbar choice :</u>
You can choose a fullscreen page toolbar instead of the little default one.
>>>>> <u>Critical Success/Failure Roll & Roll Templates Color choices :</u>
You can choose another color for critical success/failure, and roll templates header, or use the built-in rainbow color.
If you choose the rainbow, then changing the color is useless.
