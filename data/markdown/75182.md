### Description
Can you find your schedule items on your calendar only by color?
This style makes specific calendar <strong><u>underlined</u></strong> and <strong>changes shape</strong> to be recognized them very easily.
<strong><u>Select color "Mango"</u></strong> for your favorite calendar and try it.

for new look:
- underline & solid corner for orange colored items

for old look:
Orange colored schedules are changed their shapes for each view:
- "Day"/"Week"/"7Days" view : Rounded rectangle.
- "Month" view : Underline for time specified schedule, Rounded rectangle for full-day schedule,
- "Agenda" view : Underline for time specified schedule.

# Only orange colored calendar is supported now.
