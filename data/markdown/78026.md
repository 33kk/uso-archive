### Description
1. No sidebar.
2. No non-article related header, footer, ads, and (most) notices.
3. Head ruler shows search bar and 'talk' only.
4. RTL Support - Hebrew, Arabic, Farsi, etc.
5 Applies for the following wikis: wikipedia, wikibooks, wikiquote, wikisource, wikiversity, wiktionary, wikimedia.

Change the 'domain' in the script to the LTR/RTL languages you prefer (default: LTR: English, RTL: Hebrew).

A modification of the 'Wikipedia: Content only' style.
