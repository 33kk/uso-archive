### Description
A collection of style improvements for Flow-e, trying to minimise distraction and improve functionality.

- hide inbox zero image and text
- hide card author and icons on workflow
- always display actions on two lines
- lighter color for card body
- always display incomplete tasks on workflow (completed are visible on the fullscreen card)
- move due date under card date 
- move other power-ups toggles up, make them smaller and hide them until hovering on card
- taller horizontal scrollbar
