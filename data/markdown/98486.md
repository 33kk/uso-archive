### Description
A clean white theme for Firefox that removes all but the essential. Boxy borders, gradients, backgrounds, and tab boxes are all removed. Inspiration from flat design principles and iOS 7. Updated for Firefox 29 beta to remove the over-dominating curved Australis tabs.
