### Description
There is currently a group on facebook asking for a pink facebook for breast cancer awareness. To make things easy for facebook to do that (and so that people can have a pink facebook in the meantime) I made this. It turns every blue element from the facebook normal look to a similar toned pink. Also adds a breast cancer ribbon to the facebook logo.
