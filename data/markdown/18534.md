### Description
This script adds a black background and a Lava Orb image to a blank page in Firefox. 
Designed to work with LavaFox & BlackFox themes,  Firefox themes which you can get here: 
https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/user/4710003
To apply, use stylish or copy and paste into your userContent.css
