### Description
Replaces the ZeviantArt logo in DA's upper left corner with the old deviantART logo. Choice of 2011 or 2014 logostyles. Also optionally replaces the logo and text above More Like This. Does not work with the Christmas logo; does work with deviantNAV v7.
