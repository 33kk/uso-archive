### Description
More usable design for Jira projects.

*) visibility of block edges;
*) different styles of texts, data, usernames, auxiliary messages etc.;
*) little sizes economy: minWidth=820px, borders are 6px, ...;

After installation replace word "writeyourdomain" to your own domain name of Jira server.

For show of example of jira project go to https://jira.atlassian.com/browse/GHS-11516 , https://jira.atlassian.com/browse/JRA-38860?filter=-4 and similar.
