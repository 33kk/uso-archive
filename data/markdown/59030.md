### Description
Change style of unloaded/inactive tabs to have bent top right corner (image credit goes to style 56357) and grayed out favicon. Based on new option of Firefox "Don't load tabs until selected".

Ps: for users of Load Tabs Progressively https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/load-tabs-progressively/ the style is not applied properly on session restore for all tabs.
