### Description
<p><em>IMPORTANT!!! - To choose your own colors, backgrounds, theme color and options, click on the "Customize Settings" button below</em></p>

<p><strong>Features:<br>
Change Google style to a based on Material Design improving the visibility of search results. This theme allows to choose between Dark Theme and Light Theme, and have options to hide or show several page elements. Also allows to choose your own colors for links, background images for Google homepage, and search page.</strong></p>

<p><em>By default:<ul><li>Light theme is selected.</li>
<li>Orange is the color for visited links.</li>
<li>The backgrounds are those that can be seen on the xample images.</li>
</ul></em></p>
