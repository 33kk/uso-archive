### Description
A soothing gray and blue design that focuses on link scanability.  Uses Calibri and Segoe for users Vista / Office 2007 users, or Trebuchet MS for Mac or older Windows users.

* Removes unnecessary top nav and footer (top nav controls accessible by mousing over upper right).  
* Minimizes decorative elements.  
* Downscales intensity of the bright red 'unread' count for email, rss feeds, etc.  
* Dulls down read feed items
