### Description
Can remove the video controls, progress bar, & channel logo (Options below). Useful if they are in the way of a video/annotation, or if you want a cleaner look. 

**TIP: If you ever need to use the player controls, you can always double right-click in the player, and enable the "Show Controls" (or similar wording) option.
