### Description
A dark theme for Santander UK, to save your retinas from the painful white solar flare that this site embodies.

This is my first attempt at a site theme, please have mercy on me if it's not perfect :)

<b>IMPORTANT:</b> The theme only applies to <b>Personal banking</b>, any other type of account - business, select, corporate - will not be themed. Not all pages are fully themed, but they should be mostly dark.

This is purely a CSS theme, Stylish themes have no way of stealing any sort of information, it is purely a skin for the site. Check the CSS if you any doubts.
