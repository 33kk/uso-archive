### Description
Gets rid of ads and most of the other silliness at tvguide.com.  Also lets the grid resize to the full width of your browser.  You have to keep the flash box at the left if you want to be able to view details of a show.
...


Also note that the current Filterset.G seems to block the script to view details of a show.  If you use adblocker, you may want the following whitelist filter to make sure tvguide.com works (whether or not you use my cleaner):



@@http://www.tvguide.com/TVGOL/Ads/iframead.aspx*mainsect=listings
