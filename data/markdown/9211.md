### Description
Prevents History Items from showing up in your Awesome Bar list.
Easily Distinguish between Tags, Bookmarks and History items in your Awesome Bar List.

Use this style to Only Show Bookmarks, and or Tags and or History !!  How cool is that!?

This makes your Awesome Bar display only what types of items you want, and not all those history items of pages you don't remember going to.

Features and Functionality:
+ History Items will be Hidden
+ Tags will be Shown
+ Bookmarks will be Shown


Feel free to change the types being hidden, to make it custom to your own liking!

This style supports only Firefox3.
Why install an Extension when you can do it through CSS !

** You MUST Restart your browser for changes to take effect **


Applies to:
+ Awesome-Bar

Instructions:
To customize to your own liking, simply change the CSS to show or Hide the different types of entries:
- You can customize to hide/show the Tags
- You can customize to hide/show the History Items
- You can customize to hide/show the Bookmarks

Browser Versions:
+ Works only on Firefox3

Integrates with the following Styles:
+ Style 8564
+ Style 8562
+ Also works with the wonderful CyberSearch Firefox Extension!
