### Description
is piazza too dull for your exciting lifestyle? do you wish your pizza had more toppings? well then THIS is the theme for you. Take your dull white pizza and give it an Xtreme PINK theme. I have tested this extensively and use it daily so it should be pretty complete. if you find something that is hard to read, ugly, or doesn't fit the theme please let me know wherever you do that on this website. This is a heavily modified version of Piazza Dark by Muntoo. https://userstyles.org/styles/119769/piazza-dark
