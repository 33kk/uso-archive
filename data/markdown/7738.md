### Description
Centers the Tab Buttons on the Firefox Options (Preferences) window.  Works on Both Firefox 2 and Firefox 3.

Features:
- Centered Tab icons
- Better organization and appearance
- Also Applies to ALL Extension's Option pages (example: Tab Mix Plus Option Tabs will be centered too!)

Browser Versions:
- Works on Firefox3
- Works on Firefox2

Integrates with Style 7739.

Additional Information:
This Style has been tested on multiple Firefox Themes, and has worked perfectly on each.
