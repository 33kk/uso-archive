### Description
Attempts to hide all pop up boxes that ask for your consent to store cookies, such as those implemented in response to the GPDR. If you find these pop-up notices to be <strong>annoying</strong>, <strong>often misleading</strong>, and <strong>almost always intrusive</strong>, then this style is for you!
