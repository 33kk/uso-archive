### Description
This is the Mozilla Developer Network with the content background
darkened and the foreground—including links—lightened for contrast.
Some light backgrounds are kept, and dark colors are styled for their
foregrounds. The overall background is another shade of dark gray,
and its foreground is another shade of light gray. Lastly, the para-
graph widths are trimmed to a certain maximum to ease reading.
