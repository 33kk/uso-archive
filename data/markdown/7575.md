### Description
Removes and hides the Home Button icon from the Main (Navigation) Toolbar.   Gives you a larger area, and more screenspace for your browser.  Works for both Firefox 3 and Firefox 2.

Integrates with Style 7174, and Style 7572, allowing you to remove the RSS Icon, the Bookmark favorite icon, and the Throbber Button.
