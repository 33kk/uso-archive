### Description
This style brings publication information for books (publisher, date, ISBN, etc.) closer to the top of the screen.  

The size of the product info is larger and displayed in a monospaced font to make it easier for those needing to write down ISBNs.

Based on Amazon cleaner (http://userstyles.org/styles/4328)  by timepiece.
