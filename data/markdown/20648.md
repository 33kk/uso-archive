### Description
Makes the background of the Gmail inbox area white and pushes the extra inboxes from the side to the bottom.

To use:
- Choose the "Right side of the inbox" option for your Multiple Inboxes.
- When this style is applied, the multiple inboxes will be re-positioned to the bottom of the inbox to allow the RTM sidebar to show properly.
