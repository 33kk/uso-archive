### Description
Removes distracting elements from the Reddit website layout, including scoring, so you can focus on socializing instead of votes. Profile information is also hidden, to discourage stalking. Vote buttons remain visible to retain ability to participate. Works best with disabled Subreddit Styles. Should be compatible with other styles/scripts.
