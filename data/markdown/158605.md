### Update notes
<i>Latest major changes:</i> March 8th, 2019 -
- Updated Motiva Sans and Sarabun URLs.

You can now choose from 4 User Image corner styles: <b>Steam Square(Default)</b>, <b>Squircle</b>, <b>Square</b>, and <b>Rounded</b>;
And options to <b>keep the old STS fonts</b> Or <b>replace them with Motiva Sans/Arial/Helvetica</b> families
and to choose translate.php page theme between <b>Light</b>(Default) and <b>Dark</b>.

<b>What's new:</b>
- Modern Steam UI themed of the entire STS web pages.
- Move navigation bar to be fixed on the top of web pages.
- Option to change default fonts to Motiva Sans family for Latin and Sarabun family for Thai.
- Change color scheme to looks more modern.
- New styles for inputs, buttons, anchors, and etc.
- Icons for the Most Outdated and Untranslated string lists.
and much more.
