### Description
Clean many unnecessary parts of your gmail webpage footer. And moves "your account activity and account space left" to the top right. Works great with "Gmail Logo Remover" http://userstyles.org/styles/11168 . Also shows the useful links (New window, Print all, etc.) in a row.

Tested with Gmail 2.0 and Google Apps.
