### Description
Disables "Store" title top-right corner to save space.
Little addon for styles changing size of building list in <a href="http://orteil.dashnet.org/cookieclicker/">Cookie Clicker</a> game. Use when you have low screen resolution and want to see full building list. Additionally it aligns center and right horizontal separators to match vertical position. Compatible with 2.021
