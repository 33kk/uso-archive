### Description
This userstyle begins with style 854 (not updated since 2006).  It then removes tables of contents, alert boxes, footnotes, "citation needed" remarks, disambiguation notices, infoboxes, external-link icons, and anonymous-login banners.  It colors all links black, makes the font Times New Roman, and tightens the line spacing a bit.
