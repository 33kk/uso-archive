### Description
<p><strong>**** DEPRECATED ****</strong></p>
<p>Scryfall has replaced their saved cards feature and replaced it with decklists which are incompatible with a css-only solution. Click on the post below for a workaround and upvote it to make me feel good about myself.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.reddit.com/r/freemagic/comments/dcz6g2/i_created_a_new_tool_to_print_playtest_cards_from/">https://www.reddit.com/r/freemagic/comments/dcz6g2/i_created_a_new_tool_to_print_playtest_cards_from/</a></p>
<p><strong>**** DEPRECATED ****</strong></p>
<p>Appropriately positions and sizes cards on the page to print 8 Magic cards per sheet for easy printing with minimal cuts. Make sure to print in landscape!</p>
