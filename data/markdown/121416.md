### Description
A combination/blend of Black Youtube by Panos and DarkTube by Nass O
This is a dark/contrast theme focused on minimization.
Allows for widescreen canvas view of HTML5 video with width/height constraint as well as many additional element changes to minimize/reduce useless empty space.
