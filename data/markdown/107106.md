### Description
This simple dash theme comes in 10 different color palettes straight from <a href="www.colourlovers.com/palettes/most-loved/all-time/meta">Colourlovers most loved palettes of all time</a>, and features 2 different tumblr logos to choose from. The text color palette for this theme is in white/greyscale and if you want to customize it but don't know how, or want anything else about this theme changed to your tastes, message me <a href="http://www.eatyourheirs.tumblr.com/ask">@eatyourheirs</a> for personalized assistance.

<b>Check out the gradient links in additional info!</b>
