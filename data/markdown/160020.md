### Description
Give more space to read incoming mail

<b>Features:</b> Inbox mails takes only one line, Change the colors of selected/on mouse over/new mail. Change the + icon color in the left Sidebar. Change the background color and police color of the left Sidebar. Minimize the space taken at the top by the GMail icon and others. Delete the background of the account icon.

Deleted the right sidebar in order to have more place for reading mails.

You must choose a dark theme for a better effect and the dark text background mode (Settings Icon > Themes > A > Dark > Save)
