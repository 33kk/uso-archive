### Description
<p>A css theme for deviantart to make notifications surfing less painful.</p>
<p>Applies to the groups and artists notifications stack under "https://www.deviantart.com/.../art/" and "https://www.deviantart.com/.../journal/" URI prefix</p>

<p>Features:</p>
<ul><li>• Prevents "Remove from Notifications" button from going all over the place.</li>
<li>• Moves Details and Stats blocks to an eye level</li>
<li>• Hides some ads.</li></ul>

<p>Whenever that new DA UI/UX team rolls out the new design, this fix will be irrelevant.</p>
