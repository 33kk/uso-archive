### Description
Cleaner Friendfeed Remixed but with bigger text. 



It is a tweaked version of AJ Batac's Cleaner Friendfeed Beta Widescreen.

The grey is gone. The bars are white. The logo is white. Got rid of the highlighted comments. It's just a blue, white, and black color scheme. Font is still the same size. Also the entries are slightly indented so everything looks a little less jammed in the corner.

Also inspired by a mock up from Lindsey Smith.
