### Description
My most colorful KoL theme yet!
Choose which scheme you want, and have more subdued hues than blue to view. ;)

Color values are as follows: 
| Green = #59955C
| Cerulean = #007BA7
| Purple = #882266 / #872187
| Dark Red = #800000 ("darkred")
| Rust = #B7410E / #EC5800
| Olive Yellow = olive / #D1E231
