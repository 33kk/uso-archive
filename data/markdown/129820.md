### Description
Red & Black style for Outlook.com/Hotmail Login/Mail/Skype, the colors are customizable, looks great with FT DeepDark Firefox skin by steva https://addons.mozilla.org/en-GB/firefox/addon/ft-deepdark/?src=cb-dl-users.

### Update notes
Works on Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Vivaldi and possible others.

You can set the color scheme however you like, make it colorful, make it bright or make it dark.
