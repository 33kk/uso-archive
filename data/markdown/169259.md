### Description
The big torrent trackers have recently closed in Europe. This event provoked a large-scale discussion both in the Internet community and in the press. The anti-piracy wave has reached Russia. In one of the January editions of the Russian print edition devoted to life on the Internet, this issue was raised, but at that time no one knew yet whether this massive opposition to Web piracy would affect the resources of national torrents. It turned out that "problems" appeared with the largest number of registered resource <a href="https://ext.to">https://ext.to</a>
