### Description
Just makes the light twitter theme a little more minimal and a little easier on the eyes. This also overrides the <i><b>purple</b></i> display color and makes the timeline and some other things a bit wider and hides the sidebar.

This will not work on either of the dark twitter themes since I changed colors of the light theme, but the section that changes the purple display color is in a different section so it's easy to find.
