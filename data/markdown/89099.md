### Description
A wide, dark theme for youtube with a large centered player for HDTVs and large monitors. Tested and works with Chrome, Firefox, and Safari. 

NOTE:  Now works with Firefox. Tested with Firefox 23. Position of the sidebar will not be <i>quite</i> as perfectly aligned as it is in Chrome and Safari. If you want it <b>PERFECTLY</b> aligned you can get the Firefox specific version <a href="http://userstyles.org/styles/90359/youtube-bigscreen-for-firefox?r=1373539392">here</a>.
