### Description
This userstyle will increase the URLbar and Searchbox text size, making it easier to see (similar to Google Chrome).
As opposed to other userstyles, it will not change the size of the URLbar and toolbar buttons  (tested with the latest FF 4.0b8pre).


You can easily edit the style and choose the desired font-size (default is 14).
