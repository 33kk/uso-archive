### Description
For vertically challenged screens, this extension allows to gain 15px of vertical viewspace by reducing the size of the toolbars. Contrary to other extensions, it doesn't change any margins, keeping the tabs looks. Compatible with Classic Theme Restorer's Small buttons fix.
