### Description
This VERY SIMPLE style hides spoiler elements from the Netflix GUI.
Elements hidden :
 - background picture displayed during the loading of the video (major source of spoiling)
 - background picture displayed between episodes
 - rotating pictures taken from the movie/show while hovering titles
