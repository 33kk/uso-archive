### Description
This styles the TCTC Blackboard to fall more in line with the official colors of the college. It also adds some styling tweaks to fix some oversights.

<b>If you have High Contrast Mode on in Blackboard, there is a chance this style will overwrite all of those styles. Please keep this in mind before installing.</b>
