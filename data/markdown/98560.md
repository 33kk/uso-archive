### Description
This style changes the appearance of Google Docs documents providing a beautiful and distraction free writing environment. It replaces the background and sheet textures (edit to use other images), hides away the toolbar and the comments section (Thanks IanHead). Use it in fullscreen mode for best effect. Enjoy.
