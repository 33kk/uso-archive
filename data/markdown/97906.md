### Description
Removes Gmail recipient suggestions from new emails ("Add" section, formerly "consider including.")  Also removes "invite [person] to Gmail" and "share" links (for G+ users).  

This is a port of the Gmail Consider Nothing userscript.  All info, terms and conditions apply: http://userscripts.org/scripts/show/106625
