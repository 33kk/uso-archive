### Description
1. Shrink all inactive tabs to show just their favicon
2. Display title and close button on selected tab (like normal)
3. Expand shrunk tabs to show title and close button on mouse over 

*tested with Firefox 4 but may work on other versions

Recommended: use in combination with http://userstyles.org/styles/45794/ff4-urlbar-in-tabbar-ie9-style

See Version 2 with faviconized active tab: http://userstyles.org/styles/47782/ff4-minimal-tabs-expandable-faviconized-tabs-v2
