### Description
Literature & Latte forums loads the Effra font family but then does not consistently apply it, leaving a mish mash of Verdana, Tahoma, Lucida, Effra and others in various places. This userstyle returns Effra back to its rightful place. One major complaint which I think forced L&L to step back from Effra was in the editor people found comma and period hard to distinguish, due to the use of Effra-Light. This style makes sure Effra-Regular is used in input and textarea edit fields...
