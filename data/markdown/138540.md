### Description
Redesign of Blogger's post composition page. 
Choose your own colors under "Customize Settings".

### Update notes
- Customize with your own color scheme (3 colors) under "Customize"
- Defaults to "bankers' lamp" style (green and wheat)
- Serif font for blog composition.
- Brown scheme and monospace font for HTML composer 
- Rounded corners post composer (set to 0 under "Customize" to remove)
