### Description
Dark style applies to the Swedish online newspaper Aftonbladet as well as sub sites such as Sportbladet etc.
The ads are hidden by CSS. Media will play with pre-advertising hidden but the main video will show.
Easy on the eyes for night reading.
-----------------------

Läs Aftonbladet och undersidor som Sportbladet utan reklam och log in begäran.
Mörk stil som gör det bekvämt att läsa kvällstid.
