### Description
Pocket Minimalismo makes it nicer to use Pocket by hiding distractions and providing a cleaner appearance.
<br /><br /><br />

### Update notes
<b>Features</b>
<ul>
<li>A simpler and cleaner appearance.</li>
<li>Customize your level of minimalismo.</li>
<li>Many elements such as the top and left navigation are hidden but reappear when you hover your mouse over them.</li>
</ul>

<b>Notes</b>
<ul>
<li>When customizing the options, "Hide" means the element is hidden but reappears when you mouse over it. "Remove" means the element is removed entirely.</li>
</ul>
