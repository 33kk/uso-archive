<strong>Now with Australis support!</strong>  This style makes Firefox tabs look like those in <a href="http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/?content=154296">the FlatStudio GTK+ theme</a> (without the rounded corner, as when tabs-2.css is selected in gtk.css).  There is also <a href="//userstyles.org/styles/86997/thunderbird-flatstudio-tabs-like-tabs-2-css">a Thunderbird version</a>.
