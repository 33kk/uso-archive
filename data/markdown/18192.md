### Description
A 'darkening' style for white/bright websites - especially for browsing at low light (~in the night); 

unlike most of the other darkening styles on 'userstyles.org' this one won't turn the site completly black - it renders the site with dark grey backgrounds and light grey colored text for less straining contrasts. (screenshots)


update26.08.2009 adjustments concerning background-images (ie. facebookk icons), headers and paragraph background color.
