### Update notes
To import Google fonts (can't include in public style sheet due to non-chrome imports causing hangs)

Put this line at the top:

@import url("https://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=/*[[font]]*/:400,700&display=swap");

Then, replace "/*[[font]]*/ with whatever font you like.

Ex: 
@import url("https://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Comfortaa:400,700&display=swap");
