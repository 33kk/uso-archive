### Description
This userstyle is for the new version of Google Calendar (which has a muted design).

+ Removes the header menu items (that link to other Google services)
+ Removes the search bar
+ Converts text of individual calendar items to white, adds grey hover
+ Converts current day background to pale yellow, and the hours sidebar to grey/blue

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

I put this userstyle together for my own convenience (my friends and I wanted to change a few aspects of it). It uses aspects of styles created by other users. If you're interested in the original versions of the styles, see:
