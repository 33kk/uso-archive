### Description
<p>A google theme that will give you a random mlp (my little pony) logo.</p>
<p>You can find the gallery of all the logos used at <a href="https://googleponies.cyberpon3.net/">googleponies.cyberpon3.net</a> as well as generating a style from a customized list of logos.</p>
<p>Current logos thanks to  ssumppg, ThePatrollPL, ViperDash, and Owl-Parchment</p>
