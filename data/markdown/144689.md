### Description
Intelligently moves the Voting bar, View counts, Social links and Reply/Edit buttons to positions where they are always visible while you scroll through and read posts.
This means you can always view and use these buttons regardless of whether you are viewing the top or the bottom of a long post.
Increased efficiency and an easier to use Steemit.com!
