### Description
Replaces blue stars to gold stars when viewing Five Star extensions in the Extensions Manager (Add-Ons) window.


Features:
+ Addons with 5 star ratings will become gold
+ You can access these ratings through the first tab of the Extension Manager (called Get Add-ons).
+ The best extensions are easier to locate, and the stars are distinguishable between good and awesome!
+ Lets you feel like a king when looking or searching for a new add-on!
