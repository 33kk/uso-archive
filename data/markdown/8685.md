### Description
This style is for extending Glasser (http://forums.mozillazine.org/viewtopic.php?t=649449) into the Google toolbar. A custom blue semitransparent background for the "word find" words enables them to be read against dark or light backgrounds behind the transparency.  For the style to work you must make one change to a Glasser file:

%appdata%\mozilla\firefox\profiles\(click yours)\extensions\glasser@sixxgate.com\chrome\content\dwmoverlay.xul

... whew!

(Note: You can get to %appdata% by pressing windows+R or going to Start:Run and typing in %appdata% and pressing return)

Change
<toolbar id="nav-bar" glassToMe="true"/>
to
<toolbar id="gtbToolbar" glassToMe="true"/>

You have to restart firefox to see the effect.
