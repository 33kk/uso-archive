### Description
Adds a black border around photos on Flickr photostream, set, and photo pages.

Changing the background color on Flickr pages tends to break the site visually (it looks bad!). Placing a thick border around images provides the same increased contrast for photos, making them look much better, without disrupting Flickr's layout.
