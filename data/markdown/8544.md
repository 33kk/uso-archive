### Description
It combines the Stop Button and Reload Button, and put them at the right side of the Url-bar.

My first Style, just for fun.

Like others similar styles,  to make it works, customize the Firefox interface and change the position of the buttons to [ URLBAR - STOP - RELOAD], then activate the style.
