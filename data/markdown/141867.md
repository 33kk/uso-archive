Some issues with sending "to" in PM, but whatever.

#17161b = Background color
#cccccc = Default colour of text in posts/threads
#cd1818 = Theme/asccent colour
#FFDE89 = Usergroup colour (all groups)
#808080 = Colour of banned members
#a0a0a0 = Colour of someones status in postbit (eg. "Known Member" above stars)
#008000 = Color of the text stating if a member is online
#2bbd2b = Colour of positive reputation
#ff5b5b = Colour of negative reputation
#901212 = Darker shade of the theme colour
#0a0a0a = Thread seperator bar (eg. splits "Important/Stickied" threads from "Normal" ones)
#0a0a0a = Threads header bar (eg. the one with "Thread/Author | Replies | Views etc.")
#fefefe = Secondary color that comes on top of background color
