### Description
Reclaim your screen real-estate.  

The new for 2011 pitpass site redesign wastes a lot of space. Unfortunately, a lot of the layout is hardcoded and dfficult to override with css. 

This style hides the headlines/slideshow from being displayed, and defaults to a smaller font size.
