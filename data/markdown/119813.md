### Description
Customise your updates and stats bar colours and heights on MAL profiles!
Compatible with other Stylish MAL Stylesheets!
Note: the styles swap completed bar and watching bar colours (green to blue, and blue to green) as I feel it makes more sense as blue in progress and green as finished.
