### Description
A dark, stylish design inspired by Material.

**NOTE** - Apparently there is a difference between a standard "username@gmail.com" account and a Google Business account when using YouTube. All of the CSS and selectors are entirely different on a Google Business account, using a "branded" interface. My YouTube account is on my Google Business account, and I did not know this when designing this theme. This theme WILL NOT WORK with standard "username@gmail.com" accounts on YouTube. Sorry :/
