### Description
The "Remove All" buttons in various Firefox security and privacy exceptions lists immediately remove the entire list--a mistaken press is all too easy.

This style removes the "Remove All" button from the saved passwords, cookie exceptions, cookie list, pop-up window exceptions and image exceptions.
