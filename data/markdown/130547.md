### Description
This stylesheet corrects TeamDynamix's difficult to use printouts by removing unhelpful information entirely and putting client and problem info on the first page of the printout. This is designed to be used by our IT Maint, Image Center, and Service Center departments to ease tracking of systems physically at IT.
