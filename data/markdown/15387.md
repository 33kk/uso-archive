### Description
Removes the Blue Arrrow PHProxy Inbasic icon button from Firefox3's Addressbar (Locationbar).  Gives you more room to type in that long URL, or to search using the Awsomebar.

Integrates with Style 7173, and Style 7174, allowing you to remove the RSS Icon, the Bookmarks favorite icon, and the Go Button.


Additional Information:
This style works on Firefox 2 and Firefox 3
Gives you more space in the Firefox 3 Awesome Bar (found on the Navigation Toolbar)
