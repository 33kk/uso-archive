### Description
Provides a few quality-of-life improvements for the battling system on <a href="http://www.furvilla.com/">FurVilla</a>:
<br>
<ul><li>Condenses the page (less scrolling).
  <li>Opens the opponent's stats automatically.
  <li>Moves up the candycorn button; allows you to continue battling without exiting the modal.
  <li>Use your consumables at the end of the battle even when the modal is open.
  <li>Numbers the first page of the tourney leaderboard.
  <li>Moves the "Begin scrapping" button on blacksmith pages; allows you to continue scrapping without exiting the modal.
</ul>
Under Customize Settings, you can number all pages of the leaderboard, disable the sliding animation, and move the end-of-battle stats up (ideal for shorter screens).
