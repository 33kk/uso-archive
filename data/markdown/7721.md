### Description
Allows you to Customize the Text Color and Background color of the Bookmarks Toolbar buttons when hovering over them.  When your mouse is hovering over your bookmarks toolbar icons, they will be highlighted in a different custom color.  Increases screen navigation and readability.
Once again, this style supports both Firefox2 and Firefox3.


Applies to:
- Bookmarks Toolbar (text of your bookmarks)

Code provided (The Code provided will do the following):
- Hovered Bookmarks will have a Black background
- Hovered Bookmarks will have a Lime-Green Text color.
- Hovered Bookmarks will have their background image removed (so the color shows through)


Browser Versions:
- Works on Firefox 3
- Works on Firefox 2


Integrates with Style 7719.
