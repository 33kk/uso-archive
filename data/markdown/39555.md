### Description
Minimizes the addonbar to occupy minimal space in the bottom right corner.
Addonbar can be autohided if you want.

Also moves findbar and download statusbar to the bottom left and removes the addonbar close button (it’s so small now that we don’t need to show/hide it constantly)

The Screenshot shows both old, obsolete “statusbar” buttons and standard toolbar buttons.
