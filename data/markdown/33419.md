### Description
Removes the most distracting elements from the new news.google.com:

- Annoying hover effects.
- Star/share icons.
- Topic links above headlines.
- Customization links.
- Inconsistent headline styles.

Personalization options are hidden by this style. If you want to change news options, temporarily go to www.google.com/news instead of news.google.com.
